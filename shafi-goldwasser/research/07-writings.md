# Shafi Goldwasser: Selected Writings

## Lecture Notes

### "Lecture Notes on Cryptography" (2008)
**Authors**: Shafi Goldwasser, Mihir Bellare
**Content**: Comprehensive graduate-level introduction to modern cryptography.
**Impact**: Standard reference for cryptography courses.

### "The Knowledge Complexity of Interactive Proof Systems"

### "The Cryptographic Lens"
**Venue**: ACM Turing Award Lecture, 2013
**Content**: Vision for cryptography's role in computer science.
