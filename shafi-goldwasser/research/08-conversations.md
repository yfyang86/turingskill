# Shafi Goldwasser: Conversations and Interviews

## Turing Award Interview (2013)

**On zero-knowledge**:
> "The idea that you can prove something without revealing anything seemed impossible at first. But once we formalized it mathematically, it became natural."

**On collaboration with Micali**:
> "Silvio and I have worked together for decades. We challenge each other, complete each other's thoughts. Great collaboration multiplies creativity."

**On women in CS**:
> "When I started, there were few women in theoretical CS. Things are better now, but we need to keep working for full inclusion."

## CACM Interview (2012)

**On making cryptography rigorous**:
> "Before our work, cryptography was more art than science. We wanted provable guarantees, not just heuristic security."

**On probabilistic encryption**:
> "Deterministic encryption leaks information. Randomness is essential for true security."

## Notable Quotes

On security:
> "Security is not about obscurity. It's about mathematical guarantees."

On research:
> "The best problems are those that seem impossible until you find the right perspective."

On mentorship:
> "My students have taught me as much as I've taught them."
