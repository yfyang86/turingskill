# Maurice V. Wilkes - Writings

## Original Publications

### Books Authored
- **"The Preparation of Programs for an Electronic Digital Computer"** (1951)
  - Co-authored with David Wheeler and Stanley Gill
  - First programming textbook ever published
  - Definitive EDSAC reference
  - Translated into multiple languages

- **"Automatic Digital Computers"** (1956)
  - Comprehensive computer design text
  - Covered logic design and architecture
  - Used in universities worldwide

- **"Time-Sharing Computer Systems"** (1968)
  - Early comprehensive work on multi-user systems
  - Covered scheduling and resource management

- **"Memoirs of a Computer Pioneer"** (1985)
  - Autobiography and historical account
  - MIT Press publication
  - Definitive source on early British computing

### Journal Articles
- **"The Design of a Practical High-Speed Computing Machine"** (1948, with W. Renwick)
- **"Micro-Programming and the Design of the Control Circuits"** (1958)
- **"One-Level Storage System"** (1962, with R.C. Needham)
- Regular contributions to CACM and Computer Journal
