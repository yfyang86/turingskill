# Maurice V. Wilkes - Key Papers and Publications

## Foundational Books

### Programming and Computer Design
- **"The Preparation of Programs for an Electronic Digital Computer"** (1951)
  - With David Wheeler and Stanley Gill
  - First programming textbook ever published
  - EDSAC programming manual
  - Addison-Wesley

- **"Automatic Digital Computers"** (1956)
  - Comprehensive computer design text
  - Covered logic design and architecture
  - Methuen (London), Wiley (New York)

- **"Time-Sharing Computer Systems"** (1968)
  - Early work on multi-user systems
  - American Elsevier

- **"Memoirs of a Computer Pioneer"** (1985)
  - Autobiography
  - MIT Press
  - Definitive account of early British computing

## Key Technical Papers

### Early Computer Papers
- **"The Design of a Practical High-Speed Computing Machine"** (1948)
  - With W. Renwick
  - Journal of Scientific Instruments
  - EDSAC design description

- **"Programme Design for a High-Speed Automatic Calculating Machine"** (1949)
  - Journal of Scientific Instruments
  - Early programming methodology

### Microprogramming
- **"The Best Way to Design an Automatic Calculating Machine"** (1951)
  - Manchester University Computer Inaugural Conference
  - First description of microprogramming

- **"Micro-Programming and the Design of the Control Circuits in an Electronic Digital Computer"** (1958)
  - Proceedings of Cambridge Philosophical Society
  - Formal treatment of microprogramming

### Computer Architecture
- **"EDSAC 2"** (1960)
  - IEEE Transactions on Electronic Computers
  - Microprogrammed computer design

- **"One-Level Storage System"** (1962)
  - With R.C. Needham
  - Atlas/Titan virtual memory
  - IRE Transactions on Electronic Computers
