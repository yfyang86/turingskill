# Marvin Minsky - Key Papers and Publications

## Foundational Books

### Artificial Intelligence Foundations
- **"The Society of Mind"** (1986)
  - Simon & Schuster
  - 270 one-page chapters
  - Distributed intelligence theory
  - Bestseller, translated worldwide

- **"The Emotion Machine"** (2006)
  - Simon & Schuster
  - Commonsense thinking, emotions
  - Cognitive architecture
  - Resource allocation in mind

- **"Perceptrons: An Introduction to Computational Geometry"** (1969, 1988)
  - With Seymour Papert
  - MIT Press
  - Mathematical analysis of neural nets
  - Expanded edition 1988

### Computation Theory
- **"Computation: Finite and Infinite Machines"** (1967)
  - Prentice-Hall
  - Automata theory textbook
  - Turing machines, neural nets
  - Formal language theory

## Key Technical Papers

### Neural Networks and Learning
- **"Steps Toward Artificial Intelligence"** (1961)
  - Proceedings of the IRE
  - Comprehensive AI survey
  - Influenced research agenda

- **"Neural Nets and the Brain Model Problem"** (1954)
  - Ph.D. thesis, Princeton
  - Early learning theory
  - SNARC description

### Knowledge Representation
- **"A Framework for Representing Knowledge"** (1975)
  - In "The Psychology of Computer Vision" (Winston, ed.)
  - Frame theory introduced
  - Influenced OOP and KR

### Robotics and Vision
- **"Matter, Mind and Models"** (1965)
  - Proceedings of IFIP Congress
  - Philosophical foundations
  - Representation issues

- **"The Society of Mind" papers (various, 1970s-1980s)**
  - Individual chapter publications
  - Cognitive science journals
