# Marvin Minsky - Timeline

## Early Life and Education

| Year | Event |
|------|-------|
| 1927 | Born August 9, New York City |
| 1944-1945 | U.S. Navy service |
| 1950 | B.A. in Mathematics, Harvard University |
| 1951 | Built SNARC (first neural network learning machine) |
| 1954 | Ph.D. in Mathematics, Princeton University |
| 1954-1957 | Junior Fellow, Harvard Society of Fellows |

## AI Founding Era

| Year | Event |
|------|-------|
| 1956 | Co-organizes Dartmouth AI Conference |
| 1956 | Term "Artificial Intelligence" coined |
| 1957 | Patents confocal microscopy |
| 1957 | Joins MIT faculty |
| 1958 | Co-founds MIT AI Project with John McCarthy |
| 1959 | MIT AI Project becomes MIT AI Laboratory |

## Major Works and Contributions

| Year | Event |
|------|-------|
| 1961 | "Steps Toward Artificial Intelligence" published |
| 1967 | "Computation: Finite and Infinite Machines" published |
| 1969 | **Receives ACM Turing Award** |
| 1969 | "Perceptrons" published (with Papert) |
| 1975 | Frame theory paper published |
| 1980 | "Mindstorms" (Papert, with Minsky contributions) |
| 1986 | "The Society of Mind" published |
| 2006 | "The Emotion Machine" published |
