# Marvin Minsky - Writings

## Original Publications

### Books Authored
- **"The Society of Mind"** (1986)
  - Simon & Schuster
  - 270 interconnected one-page chapters
  - Theory of distributed intelligence
  - Translated into multiple languages
  - Influenced cognitive science broadly

- **"The Emotion Machine"** (2006)
  - Simon & Schuster
  - Commonsense thinking and emotions
  - Cognitive architecture theory
  - Resource allocation in thinking

- **"Perceptrons"** (1969, expanded 1988)
  - With Seymour Papert
  - MIT Press
  - Mathematical analysis of neural networks
  - Highly influential, controversial

- **"Computation: Finite and Infinite Machines"** (1967)
  - Prentice-Hall
  - Automata theory textbook
  - Turing machines, computability
