# Andrew Chi-Chih Yao - Legacy and Impact

## Theoretical Contributions

### Yao's Principle
- Fundamental tool in analysis of randomized algorithms
- Taught in every graduate algorithms course worldwide
- Thousands of papers cite and apply the principle
- Bridge between probabilistic and deterministic analysis

### Communication Complexity
- Established thriving research subfield
- Applications in VLSI, data streams, distributed computing
- Standard textbook topic (Kushilevitz-Nisan)
- Continues to yield new results decades later

## Institutional Legacy

### Tsinghua IIIS
- Created world-class research institute from scratch
- Attracted international talent to China
- Model for interdisciplinary CS education
- Trained generation of Chinese TCS researchers

### Chinese Computer Science
- Elevated China's status in theoretical CS
- Created pipeline for Chinese researchers
- Influenced CS education nationwide
- Bridge between Chinese and international communities
