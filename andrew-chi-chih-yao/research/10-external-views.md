- Communication complexity work created thriving subfield

- **Pioneer of return migration:** First major scientist to return full-time

- **Strengths:** Deep, rigorous, foundational

- **Supporters:** Brave decision, contributed to Chinese science

- **Admirers:** High standards, interdisciplinary vision
