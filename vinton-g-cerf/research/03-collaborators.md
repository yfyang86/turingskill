# Vinton G. Cerf - ucollaborators

## Overview
Vinton "Vint" Cerf is an American Internet pioneer, recognized as one of "the fathers of the Internet." He co-designed the TCP/IP protocols with Robert Kahn.

## Turing Award 2004
- Shared with Robert E. Kahn
- For pioneering work on internetworking, including the design and implementation of the Internet's basic communications protocols

## Key Information
- Born: June 23, 1943, New Haven, Connecticut
- Current: VP and Chief Internet Evangelist at Google
- Co-designed TCP/IP (1974)
- Co-founded ARPANET protocols

## Research Focus
- TCP/IP protocols
- Internet architecture
- Network protocols
- Space communications (delay-tolerant networking)
