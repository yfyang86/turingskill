# Alan Kay - Biography

## Personal Information
- **Born:** May 17, 1940, Springfield, Massachusetts, USA
- **Turing Award Year:** 2003
- **Award Citation:** For pioneering work on object-oriented programming and windowing graphical user interfaces

## Education
- University of Utah: B.S., M.S., Ph.D. (1969)
- Thesis: "The Reactive Engine"

## Career
- Xerox PARC (1970-1980): Dynabook, Smalltalk, GUI
- Apple (1984-1996): Apple Fellow
- Disney (1996-2001): Imagineering VP
- HP, Viewpoints Research Institute

## Major Achievements
- Smalltalk programming language
- Dynabook concept (personal computer vision)
- Object-oriented programming
- Windowing GUI
- Squeak, Etoys educational software

## Honors
- 2003 ACM Turing Award
- Charles Stark Draper Prize
- Multiple honorary degrees
