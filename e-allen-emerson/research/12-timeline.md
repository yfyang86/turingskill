# E. Allen Emerson: Timeline

## 1954-1976: Early Life and Education
| Year | Event | Significance |
|------|-------|--------------|
| 1954 | Born December 15, Dallas, Texas | Texas upbringing shaped modest, practical orientation |
| 1972 | Entered University of Texas at Austin | Chose UT for undergraduate studies |
| 1976 | B.S. in Mathematics, UT Austin | Mathematical foundation for later work |

## 1976-1981: Graduate Studies
| Year | Event | Significance |
|------|-------|--------------|
| 1976 | Began M.S. in Mathematics at UT Austin | Continued mathematical training |
| 1978 | M.S. in Mathematics completed | Advanced mathematical preparation |
| 1978 | Switched to Computer Science Ph.D. | Pivot toward applied formal methods |
| 1978 | Began working with Edmund Clarke | Foundational collaboration begins |
| 1981 | Ph.D. in Computer Science awarded | Dissertation on temporal logic and verification |

## 1981-1982: Breakthrough Years
| Year | Event | Significance |
|------|-------|--------------|
| 1981 | Joined UT Austin faculty as Assistant Professor | Academic career begins |
| 1981 | Clarke-Emerson model checking paper published | Foundation of the field |
| 1982 | Emerson-Clarke synthesis paper published | Extended model checking to code generation |

## 1983-1990: Establishing the Field
| Year | Event | Significance |
|------|-------|--------------|
| 1983 | POPL paper on branching vs. linear time | Definitive comparison of LTL vs. CTL |
| 1986 | Mu-calculus automata paper with Lei | Automata-theoretic foundations |
| 1987 | Promoted to Associate Professor | Tenure achieved |
| 1990 | Handbook of TCS survey on temporal logic | Became standard reference |

## 1990-2000: Mature Contributions
| Year | Event | Significance |
|------|-------|--------------|
| 1990 | Promoted to Full Professor | Senior rank achieved |
| 1990s | Symbolic model checking development | Contributed to BDD-based techniques |
| 1997 | Handbook chapter on model checking and mu-calculus | Descriptive complexity connections |
| 1999 | Mobile processes paper with Trefler | Extended to dynamic systems |

## 2000-2007: Parameterized Verification
| Year | Event | Significance |
|------|-------|--------------|
| 2000 | Parameterized protocol verification with Namjoshi | Extended beyond finite-state |
| 2000 | Ring network verification with Kahlon | Addressed arbitrary process counts |
| 2000s | Multiple papers on infinite-state systems | Pushed theoretical boundaries |
