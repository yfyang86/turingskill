# Yann LeCun - Timeline

## 1960s-1980s: Early Life and Education

### 1960
- **July 8:** Born near Paris, France

### 1983
- **Master's degree:** ESIEE Paris (Electrical Engineering)
- **Discovery:** Read about neural networks, "changed my life"

### 1983-1987
- **Ph.D. studies:** Université Pierre et Marie Curie (Paris VI)
- **Advisor:** Maurice Milgram
- **Thesis:** Learning processes in asynchronous neural networks

### 1987
- **Ph.D. awarded** in Computer Science
- **Postdoc:** University of Toronto
- **Advisor:** Geoffrey Hinton
- **Work:** Backpropagation, handwritten character recognition

## 1988-2003: Bell Labs Era

### 1988
- **Joined AT&T Bell Labs** in Holmdel, New Jersey
- **Research group:** Adaptive Systems Research Department
- **Colleagues:** Léon Bottou, Vladimir Vapnik

### 1989
- **LeNet-1 developed:** First convolutional network
- **Application:** Handwritten digit recognition

### 1993
- **Optimal Brain Damage paper** (with Denker and Solla)
- Network pruning technique

### 1995
- **LeNet-5:** Definitive CNN architecture
- **Paper:** "Comparison of Learning Algorithms"

### 1996
- **DjVu development begins:** Document compression
- **Goal:** Efficient web document distribution

### 1998
- **Gradient-Based Learning paper:** Proceedings of the IEEE
- Seminal LeNet-5 publication

### 1996-2002
- **Head of AT&T Labs Image Processing Research Department**

### 2002
- **AT&T Labs restructuring** (Lucent spin-off challenges)

## 2003-2013: NYU Period

### 2003
- **Joined New York University** (Courant Institute)
- **Position:** Professor of Computer Science

### 2008
- **IEEE Fellow** elected

### 2012
- **ImageNet breakthrough:** AlexNet validates deep learning
- **Vindication:** CNN approach proven correct

### 2013
- **Meta (Facebook) offer:** Create AI research lab
- **Founded NYU Center for Data Science**
- **Became first director**

### 2013
- **Joined Facebook** as Director of AI Research

## 2013-Present: Facebook/Meta Era

### 2013
- **FAIR founded:** Facebook AI Research
- **Mandate:** Open AI research
- **Location:** New York, Menlo Park, Paris, London

### 2015
- **PyTorch development begins**
- Open-source deep learning framework

### 2016
- **Promoted to Chief AI Scientist** (VP level)
- Expanded research mandate

### 2017
- **Promoted to Chief AI Scientist** (SVP level)

### 2018
- **ACM A.M. Turing Award** (with Hinton and Bengio)
- **Citation:** "For conceptual and engineering breakthroughs"
- **Recognition:** Deep learning "godfather" status

### 2019
- **Legion of Honor:** French national distinction
- **IEEE CIS Neural Networks Pioneer Award**

### 2021
- **AAAI Fellow** elected

### 2022
- **JEPA paper:** "A Path Towards Autonomous Machine Intelligence"
- **Princess of Asturias Award** (Scientific Research)

### 2023
- **Congressional testimony:** AI regulation
- **LLM criticism controversy:** ChatGPT comments
- **Continued JEPA research**

### 2024
- **VinFuture Prize** awarded
- **Active research continues** on world models
