# Yann LeCun - External Perspectives

## Peer Assessments

### Geoffrey Hinton on LeCun
- "Yann is the one who made convolutional networks work"
- Praised his persistence through AI winter
- "He never stopped believing in neural networks"
- Describes him as "fierce defender of his ideas"

### Yoshua Bengio on LeCun
- "Yann's conviction shaped the field"
- Acknowledges LeCun's industry impact
- "He bridges academic rigor and practical application"
- Values his mentorship of young researchers

### Yann LeCun on Himself
- Self-described as "not diplomatic enough"
- Acknowledges controversial communication style
- "I say what I think, sometimes too directly"
- Views himself as pragmatist over theorist
