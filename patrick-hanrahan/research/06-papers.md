# Patrick Hanrahan: Key Publications

## RenderMan and Shading Languages

### "A Shading Language" (1990)
**Co-authors**: Pat Hanrahan, Jim Lawson
**Publication**: SIGGRAPH 1990
**Significance**: The definitive paper on the RenderMan Shading Language, establishing the concept of programmable shaders for describing surface appearance.

### "Rendering Equation" (1986)
**Co-author**: Jim Kajiya
**Publication**: SIGGRAPH 1986
**Significance**: While primarily Kajiya's work, Hanrahan contributed to understanding the mathematical foundations of realistic rendering.

## Volume Rendering

### "Efficient Ray Tracing of Volume Data" (1990s)
**Co-authors**: Marc Levoy, Hanrahan
**Publication**: ACM Transactions on Graphics
**Significance**: Foundational work on direct volume rendering for medical and scientific visualization.

### "Light Field Rendering" (1996)
**Co-authors**: Marc Levoy, Hanrahan
**Publication**: SIGGRAPH 1996
**Significance**: Introduced light field techniques for image-based rendering, influencing computational photography and virtual reality.

## Graphics Hardware and Systems

### "Brook for GPUs: Stream Computing on Graphics Hardware" (2004)
**Co-authors**: Ian Buck, Tim Foley, et al., Hanrahan
**Publication**: SIGGRAPH 2004
**Significance**: Pioneered general-purpose computing on GPUs, directly influencing CUDA and OpenCL.

### "A Real-Time Procedural Shading System for Programmable Graphics Hardware" (2001)
**Co-authors**: Kekoa Proudfoot, William Mark, Svetoslav Tzvetkov, Hanrahan
**Publication**: SIGGRAPH 2001
**Significance**: Demonstrated real-time programmable shading on early GPUs.

## Books

### *Physically Based Rendering* (2004, 2nd ed. 2010, 3rd ed. 2016)
**Co-authors**: Matt Pharr, Wenzel Jakob
**Publisher**: Morgan Kaufmann
**Significance**: The definitive textbook on realistic rendering. Won a Scientific and Technical Academy Award in 2014 for its impact on the film industry.

## Data Visualization

### Tableau Research Publications
- Multiple papers on visual query languages
- Interactive visualization systems
- Direct manipulation interfaces for data analysis

## Other Significant Papers

### "Curve and Surface Reconstruction from Regular and Semi-Regular Samples" (2000s)
Work on geometry processing and surface reconstruction.

### "Rendering Synthetic Objects into Legacy Photographs" (2011)
**Co-authors**: Kevin Karsch, et al., Hanrahan
**Publication**: SIGGRAPH Asia 2011
**Significance**: Techniques for seamlessly integrating 3D objects into existing photographs.

## Citation Impact

Hanrahan's papers are among the most cited in computer graphics:
- RenderMan and shading language papers: thousands of citations
- Brook for GPUs: foundational for GPU computing
- Light field rendering: influenced computational photography

## Publication Philosophy

Hanrahan's publication record reflects:
- Focus on systems that work in practice, not just theory
- Collaboration with students (over 50 PhD advisees)
- Bridging academic research and industrial applications
- Long-term impact over quantity of publications
