# Patrick Hanrahan - Research Philosophy

## Core Beliefs

### Theory Meets Practice
- Research must solve real problems
- Theoretical work grounded in practical applications
- Build systems, not just algorithms
- Publications should include working implementations

### Interdisciplinary Approach
- Graphics sits at intersection of math, physics, art, and engineering
- Collaborate across disciplinary boundaries
- Learn from practitioners in application domains
- Bring graphics techniques to new fields

## Research Methodology

### Systems Building
- Real systems reveal real problems
- Implementation forces clarity of thinking
- Open-source releases for community benefit
- Reproducible research through code sharing

### Problem-Driven Research
- Start with important unsolved problems
- Film industry needs drove RenderMan
- Scientific visualization needs drove volume rendering
- Business needs drove Tableau

### Measuring Impact
- Success = adoption by practitioners
- Citations matter, but usage matters more
- Technology transfer to industry
- Training students who advance the field

## Teaching Philosophy

### Learning by Doing
- Students should build complete systems
- Course projects with real deliverables
- Industry internships for practical experience
- Research from day one in PhD program

### Mentorship
- Long-term relationships with students
- Support career development beyond graduation
- Alumni network as extended research family
- Success of students as primary metric

## On Graphics and Art

### Technical- Creative Bridge
- Graphics enables new forms of artistic expression
- Technical excellence serves creative vision
- Tools should be invisible to artists
- Balance power with usability

### Democratization
- Professional tools should be accessible
- Reduce barriers to entry
- Tableau mission: data viz for everyone
- Open source when possible

## Views on Industry

### Academic-Industrial Collaboration
- Industry provides real problems and resources
- Academia provides long-term research and training
- Symbiotic relationship essential
- IP should not block research dissemination

### Entrepreneurship
- Tableau experience validated academic research
- Commercial success enables broader impact
- Responsible entrepreneurship includes ethics
- Giving back through philanthropy and mentorship

## Future of Graphics

### Current Thinking
- Machine learning transforming graphics
- Neural representations replacing traditional models
- Real-time photorealism becoming standard
- New display technologies requiring new algorithms

### Research Priorities
- Sustainable computing (green graphics)
- Accessibility and inclusion
- Ethics of synthetic media
- Intersection of graphics and AI

## Personal Style
- Collaborative rather than competitive
- Generous with credit and ideas
- Values clarity in communication
- Believes in field over individual glory
