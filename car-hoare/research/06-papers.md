# Tony Hoare: Key Publications

## Foundational Papers

### "Quicksort" (1961-1962)
**Computer Journal**
The sorting algorithm that changed computing.

### "An Axiomatic Basis for Computer Programming" (1969)
**Communications of the ACM**
Hoare Logic; foundational for program verification.

### "Communicating Sequential Processes" (1978)
**CACM**
Process algebra for concurrency.

## Books

### "Communicating Sequential Processes" (1985)
**Prentice-Hall**
Definitive treatment of CSP.

### "Unifying Theories of Programming" (1998)
**With He Jifeng**
Common semantic framework.

## Turing Award Lecture
