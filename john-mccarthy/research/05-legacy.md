# John McCarthy - Legacy and Impact

## Scientific Legacy

### Artificial Intelligence
- Co-founded AI field (1956)
- "Artificial Intelligence" term creator
- Symbolic AI paradigm
- Logical AI approach
- AI as rigorous discipline

### LISP Programming Language
- **Still in use:** Second oldest language (after FORTRAN)
- **Dialects:** Common Lisp, Scheme, Clojure, Emacs Lisp
- **Influence:** JavaScript, Python, Ruby
- **Features:** Garbage collection, dynamic typing, macros
- **AI Research:** Still used in AI research

### Time-Sharing Systems
- Pioneer of interactive computing
- Cloud computing precursor
- Modern OS foundation
- Multiple user resource sharing

### AI Formalisms
- **Situation calculus:** Action reasoning
- **Circumscription:** Non-monotonic reasoning
- **Frame problem:** Formalized key AI problem
- **Commonsense reasoning:** Formal approaches

## Institutional Legacy

### Stanford AI Laboratory (SAIL)
- Founded 1965
- Major AI research center
- Trained generation of AI researchers
- Innovative research culture
- Model for AI labs worldwide

### MIT AI Laboratory
- Co-founded with Minsky (1958)
- Established MIT as AI center
- Collaborative with Stanford
- Both labs highly influential

## Books and Publications

### Key Papers
- **"Recursive Functions of Symbolic Expressions"** (1960)
  - LISP definition
  - Most cited paper

- **"Programs with Common Sense"** (1958-1959)
  - AI reasoning foundation

- **"Situations, Actions and Causal Laws"** (1963)
  - Situation calculus

- **"Circumscription"** (1980)
  - Non-monotonic reasoning
