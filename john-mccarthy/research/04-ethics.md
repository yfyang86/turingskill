# John McCarthy - Ethics and Philosophy

## Philosophy of AI

### Logical AI Approach
- Intelligence can be formalized logically
- Symbolic manipulation is key
- Commonsense reasoning formalizable
- Rejected biological essentialism

### Views on Consciousness
- Machines can be conscious in principle
- No fundamental barrier to machine intelligence
- Logical AI can achieve human-level intelligence
- Skeptical of neural networks (later modified views)

## Political Philosophy

### Libertarian Views
- Advocate for individual liberty
- Limited government
- Free market economics
- Personal responsibility

### Sustainability and Progress
- Advocated for human space colonization
- Resource sustainability through technology
- Optimistic about solving problems
- Technology as solution, not problem

### Free Speech Advocacy
- Strong supporter of free expression
- Engaged in political debates
- Blogged on controversial topics
- Valued intellectual freedom

## Professional Ethics

### Research Integrity
- Logical rigor in research
- Acknowledged AI limitations
- Honest about progress and challenges
- Gave credit to collaborators

### Open Research
- Supported open sharing of ideas
- LISP made freely available
- Published extensively
- Collaborative approach

## Technology and Society

### Computing for All
- Time-sharing for accessibility
- LISP for AI research

- Educational mission

### AI Safety and Ethics
- Generally optimistic about AI

- Logical approach to AI safety
