# John McCarthy - Timeline

## Early Life and Education

| Year | Event |
|------|-------|
| 1927 | Born September 4, Boston, Massachusetts |
| 1944 | Moved to Los Angeles |
| 1948 | B.S. in Mathematics, Caltech |
| 1951 | Ph.D. in Mathematics, Princeton University |

## Early Academic Career

| Year | Event |
|------|-------|
| 1951-1953 | Instructor, Princeton University |
| 1953-1955 | Assistant Professor, Stanford University |
| 1955-1958 | Assistant Professor, Dartmouth College |
| 1956 | Co-organizes Dartmouth AI Conference; coins "Artificial Intelligence" |

## MIT Years

| Year | Event |
|------|-------|
| 1958 | Joins MIT; creates LISP |
| 1958 | Co-founds MIT AI Lab with Marvin Minsky |
| 1960 | "Recursive Functions of Symbolic Expressions" published |
| 1962 | Leaves MIT for Stanford |

## Stanford AI Laboratory

| Year | Event |
|------|-------|
| 1962 | Joins Stanford University |
| 1963 | "Situations, Actions and Causal Laws" (situation calculus) |
| 1965 | Founds Stanford AI Laboratory (SAIL) |
| 1971 | **Receives ACM Turing Award** |
| 1980 | "Circumscription" paper published |
| 2000 | Retires as Professor Emeritus |

## Awards and Recognition

| Year | Award |
|------|-------|
| 1971 | ACM Turing Award |
| 1988 | Kyoto Prize |
| 1990 | Member, National Academy of Engineering |
| 1990 | Member, National Academy of Sciences |
| 2003 | Benjamin Franklin Medal |
| 2011 | IEEE Intelligent Systems' AI's Hall of Fame |

## Later Years

| Year | Event |
|------|-------|
| 1990s-2000s | Active in political blogging |
| 1990s-2011 | Continued AI research and writing |
| 2011 | Died October 24, Stanford, California (age 84) |
