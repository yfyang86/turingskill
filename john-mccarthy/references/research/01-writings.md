# John McCarthy - Writings

## Original Publications

### Foundational Papers
- **"Recursive Functions of Symbolic Expressions"** (1960)
  - Communications of the ACM
  - Definitive LISP paper
  - Most influential work

- **"Programs with Common Sense"** (1958-1959)
  - Teddington Conference proceedings
  - AI reasoning foundation

- **"Circumscription - A Form of Non-Monotonic Reasoning"** (1980)
  - Artificial Intelligence journal
  - Formal default reasoning

### Books and Manuals
- **LISP 1.5 Programmer's Manual** (1962, 1965)
  - With MIT Computation Center
  - Standard LISP reference
