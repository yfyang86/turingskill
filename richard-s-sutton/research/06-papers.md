# Richard S. Sutton - Key Publications

## Foundational Papers

### "Temporal Difference Learning" (1988)
- **Authors**: R.S. Sutton, A.G. Barto
- **Book Chapter**: Learning and Computational Neuroscience
- **Significance**: Comprehensive TD treatment

### "Learning to Predict" (1988)
- **Author**: R.S. Sutton
- **Venue**: Machine Learning
- **Significance**: TD methods formalized

### "Integrated Architectures for Learning, Planning, and Reacting" (1991)
- **Author**: R.S. Sutton
- **Venue**: Machine Learning
- **Significance**: Dyna architecture

### "TD(λ) Converges" (1988)
- **Authors**: P. Dayan, T. Sejnowski, R.S. Sutton
- **Significance**: Convergence proof
