# Alan Kay - Interviews and Dialogues

## On OOP
"The big idea is messaging. The Japanese have a small word - ma - for the space between things. That's where the magic happens."

## On Modern Computing
"We have the equivalent of Roman numerals in computing. The real revolution hasn't happened."

## On Education
"Children are the future. If we don't give them powerful tools, we're failing."

## On Xerox PARC
"We were trying to amplify human reach, not replace it."
