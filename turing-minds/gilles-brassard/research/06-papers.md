# Gilles Brassard: Key Publications

## Foundational Papers

### "Quantum Cryptography: Public Key Distribution and Coin Tossing" (1984)
**Authors**: Charles H. Bennett, Gilles Brassard
**Publication**: Proceedings of IEEE International Conference on Computers, Systems, and Signal Processing
**Significance**: The BB84 protocol
**Impact**: Founded quantum cryptography

### "Teleporting an Unknown Quantum State via Dual Classical and Einstein-Podolsky-Rosen Channels" (1993)
**Authors**: Charles H. Bennett, Gilles Brassard, Claude Crépeau, Richard Jozsa, Asher Peres, William K. Wootters
**Publication**: Physical Review Letters
**Significance**: Quantum teleportation
**Impact**: Enabled quantum communication

### "Purification of Noisy Entanglement and Faithful Teleportation via Noisy Channels" (1996)
**Authors**: Charles H. Bennett, Gilles Brassard, Sandu Popescu, Benjamin Schumacher, John A. Smolin, William K. Wootters
**Significance**: Entanglement distillation
