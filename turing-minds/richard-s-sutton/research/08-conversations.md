# Richard S. Sutton: Interviews and Dialogues

## On Temporal Difference Learning

> "TD learning is about learning from the difference between predictions. It's how we learn to predict the future, and it's how animals learn too."

## On the Bitter Lesson

> "The bitter lesson is that human knowledge and handcrafted features ultimately lose to general methods that leverage computation."

## On Reinforcement Learning

> "RL is the computational theory of intelligence—understanding how agents learn optimal behavior through environment interaction."

## On Barto

> "Andy was my PhD advisor and collaborator for over 40 years. His insight into the psychology of learning shaped our work together."

## On Industry

> "Working at DeepMind and now Keen Technologies keeps me connected to real applications while pursuing fundamental research."

## On AGI

> "Reinforcement learning is the path to artificial general intelligence. Learning from experience is the essence of intelligence."

## Advice to Researchers

> "Focus on general principles. The specific applications will come, but understanding fundamentals endures."
