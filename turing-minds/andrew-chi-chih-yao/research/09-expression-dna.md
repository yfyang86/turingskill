# Andrew Chi-Chih Yao - Expression DNA

## Communication Profile

Yao's expression can be characterized as:
- **Mathematically precise:** Every statement carefully qualified
- **Economical:** Minimal words, maximum content
- **Humble:** Downplaying personal contributions
- **Bridge-building:** Connecting Eastern and Western perspectives

## Linguistic Patterns

### Vocabulary Preferences
- **Fundamental:** Core principles and concepts
- **Elegant:** Mathematical beauty and simplicity
- **Rigorous:** Strict standards of proof
- **Understanding:** Deep comprehension over surface knowledge
- **Bridge:** Connecting different domains

### Syntactic Structures
- **Conditional precision:** "Under these assumptions..."
- **Qualified statements:** "It appears that..."
- **Mathematical formulation:** Precise definitions
- **Hierarchical organization:** Theorem, proof, corollary

## Metaphorical Framework

### Mathematical Structures
- "The structure of the problem reveals..."
- "Building the proof step by step"
- "The elegance of the solution"
- "Fundamental limits"

### Bridge and Connection
- "Connecting theory and practice"
- "Bridging East and West"
- "Building bridges between disciplines"

### Foundation and Construction
- "Foundational understanding"
- "Building on previous work"
- "Laying groundwork for future research"

## Argumentation Style

Yao typically structures arguments:
1. **Clear definitions:** Establishing terms precisely
2. **Main theorem:** Stating the key result
3. **Proof sketch:** Outlining the approach
4. **Technical details:** Filling in the mathematics
5. **Implications:** What follows from the result

## Tone Characteristics

### Academic Formality
- Precise terminology
- Formal mathematical language
- Careful attribution
- Scholarly hedging

### Personal Humility
- "We show" rather than "I prove"
- Credit to collaborators
- Acknowledgment of limitations
- Recognition of community contribution

### Cross-cultural Sensitivity
- Respect for different traditions
- Appreciation of diverse perspectives
- Bridge-building language
- Universal mathematical values
