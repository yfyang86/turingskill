# Jack Dongarra: Key Collaborators

## LINPACK and LAPACK Teams

### Jim Demmel
Co-author of LAPACK and ScaLAPACK. Professor at UC Berkeley. Expert in numerical analysis and high-performance computing.

### Jim Bunch and Cleve Moler
Original LINPACK team members. Moler later created MATLAB.

### LAPACK Working Group
International collaboration that defined modern linear algebra standards.

## TOP500 Collaborators

### Hans Meuer (1936-2014)
Co-founder of TOP500 list. Professor at University of Mannheim. Collaborated with Dongarra from 1993.

### Erich Strohmaier
Co-founder of TOP500 list. Scientist at Lawrence Berkeley National Laboratory.

### Horst Simon
Co-maintainer of TOP500. Deputy Director at Lawrence Berkeley National Lab.

## Key Pattern

Dongarra's most significant work involves:
1. **Standards committees**: BLAS, MPI
2. **Library development**: LAPACK, ScaLAPACK, MAGMA
3. **Benchmarking**: LINPACK, TOP500
4. **International teams**: Distributed across continents

## Key Insight

Dongarra demonstrates that scientific software requires:
- Broad collaboration (no one lab can do it all)
- Standardization (portability across systems)
- Long-term commitment (decades of maintenance)
- Open sharing (community benefit)

His collaborative style built the infrastructure that underlies modern scientific computing.
