# Avi Wigderson: Key Publications

## Foundational Papers

### "Hardness vs. Randomness" (1994)
**Authors**: Noam Nisan, Avi Wigderson
**Publication**: Journal of Computer and System Sciences
**Significance**: Established the hardness-randomness paradigm
**Impact**: Foundation for derandomization theory

Key result: If certain functions are hard to compute, randomness can be efficiently simulated.

### "P = BPP if E Requires Exponential Circuits" (1997)
**Authors**: Russell Impagliazzo, Avi Wigderson
**Publication**: Journal of Computer and System Sciences
**Significance**: Complete derandomization from hardness
**Impact**: Major step toward showing P = BPP

### "Entropy Waves, the Zig-Zag Graph Product, and New Constant-Degree Expanders" (2002)
**Authors**: Omer Reingold, Salil Vadhan, Avi Wigderson
**Publication**: Annals of Mathematics
**Significance**: Novel expander construction
**Impact**: Led to SL = L breakthrough
