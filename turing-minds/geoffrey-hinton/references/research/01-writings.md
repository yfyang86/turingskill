# Geoffrey Hinton: Selected Writings and Resources

## Books and Edited Volumes

### Parallel Distributed Processing: Explorations in the Microstructure of Cognition (1986)
- **Authors:** Rumelhart, McClelland, and the PDP Research Group
- **Publisher:** MIT Press
- **Significance:** The two-volume work that launched connectionism; Hinton contributed multiple chapters
- **Contents:** Foundational papers on distributed representations, backpropagation, and neural network applications

### Deep Learning (2016)
- **Authors:** Goodfellow, Bengio, and Courville
- **Publisher:** MIT Press
- **Hinton's role:** Wrote foreword; many chapters cite his work extensively
- **Significance:** Standard graduate textbook; acknowledges Hinton's foundational contributions

## Selected Technical Reports and White Papers

### The Forward-Forward Algorithm: Some Preliminary Investigations (2022)
- **Author:** Geoffrey Hinton
- **Venue:** arXiv:2212.13345
- **Status:** Working paper proposing alternative to backpropagation

### How to Represent Part-Whole Hierarchies in a Neural Network (2021)
- **Author:** Geoffrey Hinton
- **Venue:** arXiv:2102.12627
- **Status:** Detailed proposal for GLOM architecture

### Geoffrey Hinton's Roadmap to Human-Level AI (2020)
- **Type:** Interview and technical discussion
- **Source:** Various AI podcasts and publications
- **Content:** Hinton's predictions and pathway to AGI

## Popular Science and Expository Writing

### Deep Learning - A Technology With the Potential to Transform Health Care (2019)
- **Co-authors:** Rajpurkar, Chen, Banerjee, Topol
- **Venue:** JAMA
- **Content:** Non-technical explanation of deep learning's medical applications

### What is the Right Code for Speech and Language? (2018)
- **Venue:** Cognitive Science talk
- **Content:** Historical perspective on representation learning

### Deep Learning (2015)
- **Co-authors:** Yann LeCun, Yoshua Bengio
- **Venue:** Nature
- **Content:** Review article introducing deep learning to general scientific audience
- **Impact:** Most accessible overview of the field at peak of resurgence
