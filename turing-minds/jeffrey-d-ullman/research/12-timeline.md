## Career Periods

### Education (1963-1966)
- Princeton University
- Met Alfred Aho
- PhD in Computer Science

### Bell Labs (1966-1969)
- Compiler algorithms with Aho
- Began Dragon Book work
- Unix environment

### Princeton (1969-1979)
- Automata and formal languages
- Frustration with CS marginalization
- Early database theory

### Stanford (1979-present)
- Database theory founder
- InfoLab creation
- Textbook updates
- Gradiance founder
