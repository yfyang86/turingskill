## IEEE 754 and Floating-Point Standard

**Background (1970s)**
- Inconsistent floating-point across vendors
- Numerical instability problems
- Portability challenges
- Need for standardization

**IEEE 754 Working Group**
- Led floating-point standardization effort
- 754-1985 standard published
- IEEE 754-2008 revision
- Current IEEE 754-2019

**Key Contributions**
- Gradual underflow (denormal numbers)
- NaN (Not a Number) handling
- Proper rounding modes
- Exception handling design

**Impact**
- Universal floating-point representation
- Portability guaranteed
- Numerical reliability improved
- Hardware implementation standard
