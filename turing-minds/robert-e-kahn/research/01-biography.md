# Robert E. Kahn - ubiography

## Overview
Robert Elliot Kahn is an American electrical engineer and Internet pioneer. He co-invented the TCP/IP protocols with Vint Cerf.

## Turing Award 2004
- Shared with Vinton G. Cerf
- For pioneering work on internetworking

## Key Information
- Born: December 23, 1938, Brooklyn, New York
- Founded CNRI (Corporation for National Research Initiatives)
- Co-designed TCP/IP with Cerf
- Developed packet switching concepts

## Research Focus
- TCP/IP protocols
- Internet architecture
- Information infrastructure
- Digital object architecture
