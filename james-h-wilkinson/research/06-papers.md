# James H. Wilkinson - Key Papers and Publications

## Foundational Books

### Numerical Analysis Classics
- **"Rounding Errors in Algebraic Processes"** (1963)
  - Prentice-Hall, Notes on Applied Science No. 32
  - HMSO (Her Majesty's Stationery Office)
  - Comprehensive error analysis
  - Backward error analysis foundation
  - Still referenced today

- **"The Algebraic Eigenvalue Problem"** (1965)
  - Oxford University Press
  - Definitive treatment of eigenvalue computation
  - 662 pages of rigorous analysis
  - Numerical Algorithms Group (NAG) influenced
  - Standard graduate text for decades

- **"Handbook for Automatic Computation, Vol. II: Linear Algebra"** (1971)
  - With C. Reinsch
  - Springer-Verlag
  - Algorithms in ALGOL
  - Practical implementations
  - Software standard reference

### Other Books
- **"Linear Algebra"** (Handbook Volume II contribution)
- Various edited volumes and contributions

## Foundational Papers

### Backward Error Analysis
- **"Error Analysis of Direct Methods of Matrix Inversion"** (1961)
  - Journal of the ACM
  - Early backward error analysis

- **"Rounding Errors in Algebraic Processes"** (original paper, 1960)
  - Proceedings of IFIP Congress
  - Introduced key concepts

### Eigenvalue Algorithms
- **"The QR Algorithm for Real Symmetric Matrices with Multiple Eigenvalues"** (1962)
  - Computer Journal
  - With various co-authors over years

- **"Convergence of the LR, QR, and Related Algorithms"** (1965)
  - Numerische Mathematik

### Stability Analysis
- **"The Perfidious Polynomial"** (1984)
  - Studies in Numerical Analysis, MAA
  - Wilkinson's polynomial discussion
  - Ill-conditioning examples
