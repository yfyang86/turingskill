# James H. Wilkinson - Timeline

## Early Life and Education

| Year | Event |
|------|-------|
| 1919 | Born September 27, Strood, Kent, England |
| 1930 | Scholarship to Sir Joseph Williamson's Mathematical School |
| 1939 | B.A. in Mathematics, Trinity College, Cambridge |
| 1939-1940 | Graduate studies interrupted by war |

## Military Service and Early Career

| Year | Event |
|------|-------|
| 1940-1943 | Royal Army Service Corps |
| 1943-1946 | Mathematics Division, Ministry of Supply |
| 1946 | Joins National Physical Laboratory (NPL) |
| 1946-1948 | Works with Alan Turing at NPL |

## NPL Career and Major Works

| Year | Event |
|------|-------|
| 1946-1980 | Chief Scientific Officer, NPL Mathematics Division |
| 1951 | Begins systematic error analysis research |
| 1957 | Begins visiting professorships |
| 1960 | "Rounding Errors" original paper at IFIP |
| 1963 | "Rounding Errors in Algebraic Processes" published |
| 1965 | "The Algebraic Eigenvalue Problem" published |
| 1970 | **Receives ACM Turing Award** |
| 1971 | "Handbook for Automatic Computation, Vol. II" (with Reinsch) |
| 1971-1986 | Chairman, IFIP Working Group 2.5 |

## Awards and Recognition

| Year | Award |
|------|-------|
| 1969 | Fellow of the Royal Society (FRS) |
| 1970 | ACM Turing Award |
| 1973 | John von Neumann Lecture (SIAM) |
| 1977 | A.M. Turing Award Lecture delivered |

## Later Career

| Year | Event |
|------|-------|
| 1977-1986 | Professor and Head of Numerical Analysis Group, NPL |
| 1980-1986 | Senior Visiting Fellow, Stanford University (summers) |
| 1984 | "The Perfidious Polynomial" published |
| 1986 | Died October 5, Teddington, England (age 67) |
