# James H. Wilkinson - External Perspectives

## Colleague Perspectives

### NPL Colleagues
- **Leslie Fox:** "Jim transformed numerical analysis. His backward error analysis was revolutionary - the right way to understand computation."
- **C. Reinsch:** "Working with Jim was a privilege. His clarity of thought and generosity with ideas were extraordinary."
- **Various NPL mathematicians:** "Patient mentor, brilliant analyst, humble despite achievements"

### Alan Turing Connection
- **Alan Turing (1946-1948):** Worked together at NPL on ACE. Wilkinson later acknowledged Turing's influence on his computational thinking.
