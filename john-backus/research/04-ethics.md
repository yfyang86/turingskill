FORTRAN development exemplified:
- Rigorous testing before release
- Honest performance claims
- Acknowledgment of limitations
- Commitment to reliability

## Standards Participation
ALGOL committee work:
- International cooperation
- Seeking consensus
- Technical over commercial interests
- Willingness to learn from others

Balancing corporate and community interests:
- Creating proprietary software (FORTRAN)
- Also contributing to standards (ALGOL, BNF)
- Open publication of ideas
- Teaching at universities while at IBM

Backus's famous quotes show humility:

- Admissions of many failures
- Willingness to critique own work (von Neumann style)
