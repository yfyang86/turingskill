## Writing Style

### Characteristics
- Witty and aphoristic
- Mathematical precision
- Accessible to broad audience
- Philosophical undertones
- Memorable quotations

### Recurring Themes
- Programming as intellectual discipline
- Importance of language design
- Mathematics of computation
- Education and pedagogy
- Beauty in algorithms
