# Jeffrey D. Ullman - Awards and Honors

## Turing Award (2020)
- **Award**: ACM A.M. Turing Award
- **Shared with**: Alfred V. Aho
- **Citation**: "For fundamental algorithms and theory underlying programming language implementation and for synthesizing these results and those of others in their highly influential books, which educated generations of computer scientists"
- **Significance**: Highest honor in computing
- **Monetary Award**: $1 million (shared)

### Turing Award Lecture
- Title: "Abstraction, Efficiency, and Correctness"
- Delivered virtually (COVID-19)
- Emphasized formal methods and education
- Joint presentation with Aho
