# Edgar F. Codd - Writings and Publications

## Major Books

### Cellular Automata (1968)
- **Publisher**: Academic Press, New York
- **Pages**: 125
- **ISBN**: 978-0121818500
- **Content**: Expanded PhD thesis on self-replicating automata
- **Significance**: Early theoretical computer science work
- **Availability**: Out of print, rare collector's item

### The Relational Model for Database Management: Version 2 (1990)
- **Publisher**: Addison-Wesley Professional
- **Pages**: 538
- **ISBN**: 978-0201141924
- **Content**: Comprehensive treatment of relational theory
- **Chapters**: 24 chapters covering all aspects of relational model
- **Status**: Definitive technical reference
