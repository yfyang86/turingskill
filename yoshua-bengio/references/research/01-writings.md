# Yoshua Bengio - Key Writings and Publications

## Foundational Papers

### "Neural Networks for Speech and Sequence Recognition" (1993)
- **Publisher:** John Wiley & Sons
- **Content:** Early neural network applications
- **Significance:** Pre-deep learning era work

### "Learning Long-Term Dependencies with Gradient Descent is Difficult" (1994)
- **Co-authors:** Yann LeCun, Patrice Simard, Paolo Frasconi
- **Journal:** IEEE Transactions on Neural Networks
- **Significance:** Identified vanishing gradient problem
- **Impact:** Led to LSTM, GRU development

### "A Neural Probabilistic Language Model" (2003)
- **Journal:** Journal of Machine Learning Research
- **Significance:** Neural approach to language modeling
- **Innovation:** Word embeddings concept
- **Impact:** Foundation for word2vec, modern NLP

## Deep Learning Breakthroughs

### "Greedy Layer-Wise Training of Deep Networks" (2007)
- **Co-authors:** Pascal Lamblin, Dan Popovici, Hugo Larochelle

- **Impact:** Enabled deeper networks

### "Deep Learning" (2015)

- **Significance:** Formalized representation learning field
- **Impact:** Theoretical foundations
