# Yoshua Bengio: Ethics and Professional Responsibility

## Responsible AI Development
As deep learning achieved mainstream success, Bengio became a leading voice for responsible AI:

### Montreal Declaration for Responsible AI
Bengio was a key figure in developing the Montreal Declaration, which established principles for:
- Well-being
- Respect for autonomy
- Protection of privacy and intimacy
- Solidarity
- Democratic participation
- Equity
- Diversity inclusion
- Prudence
- Responsibility
- Sustainable development

### CIFAR AI and Society Program
Bengio has been involved in CIFAR programs addressing AI's social implications.
