# Edsger W. Dijkstra - Key Papers and Publications

## Foundational Papers

### Algorithms
- **"A Note on Two Problems in Connexion with Graphs"** (1959)
  - Numerische Mathematik, Vol. 1
  - Dijkstra's algorithm published
  - Shortest path problem solved
  - Most cited paper

### Concurrent Programming
- **"Cooperating Sequential Processes"** (1965)
  - In "Programming Languages" (F. Genuys, ed.)
  - Semaphores introduced
  - Dining philosophers problem
  - Fundamental to OS

### Structured Programming
- **"Go To Statement Considered Harmful"** (1968)
  - Letter to Communications of the ACM, Vol. 11, No. 3
  - Sparked major controversy
  - Changed programming practice
  - Most famous letter in CS

### Guarded Commands
- **"Guarded Commands, Nondeterminacy and Formal Derivation of Programs"** (1975)
  - Communications of the ACM, Vol. 18, No. 8
  - if-fi and do-od constructs
  - Nondeterministic programming
  - Influenced language design
