# Edsger W. Dijkstra - External Perspectives

## Colleague Perspectives

### Dutch Computing Pioneers
- **Adriaan van Wijngaarden:** "Edsger combined mathematical brilliance with uncompromising intellectual integrity. His influence on Dutch computing was profound."
- **Jaap Zonneveld:** "Working with him on ALGOL was a masterclass in precision and elegance."
- **Various Mathematisch Centrum colleagues:** Demanding but brilliant collaborator

### International Colleagues
- **Tony Hoare:** "Dijkstra taught the field to think clearly about concurrent programming and program correctness. His influence is everywhere."
- **Donald Knuth:** Respected his algorithmic insights; debated structured programming but maintained mutual respect
- **Niklaus Wirth:** Language design discussions; shared appreciation for elegance
- **David Gries:** "Transformed how I think about programming and teaching"

## Student Perspectives

### PhD Students
- **W.H.J. Feijen:** "Demanding, precise, inspiring. Learned what it means to do computing science."
- **Jayadev Misra:** "His intellectual rigor shaped my entire career"
- **Various students:** Mixed reactions - some found him too demanding, others deeply inspired

### General Student Body
