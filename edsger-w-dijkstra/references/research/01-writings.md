# Edsger W. Dijkstra - Writings

## Original Publications

### Books Authored
- **"A Discipline of Programming"** (1976)
  - Prentice-Hall
  - Weakest precondition calculus
  - Formal program verification
  - Program derivation methods

- **"Selected Writings on Computing: A Personal Perspective"** (1982)
  - Springer-Verlag
  - Collected EWDs and papers
  - Historical and philosophical

- **"Predicate Calculus and Program Semantics"** (1990)
  - With Carel S. Scholten
  - Springer
  - Formal semantics

### Foundational Papers
- **"A Note on Two Problems in Connexion with Graphs"** (1959)
  - Numerische Mathematik
  - Dijkstra's algorithm

- **"Cooperating Sequential Processes"** (1965)
  - Semaphores introduced

- **"Go To Statement Considered Harmful"** (1968)
  - Communications of the ACM
  - Famous letter

- **"Guarded Commands, Nondeterminacy and Formal Derivation"** (1975)
  - Communications of the ACM

## Writing Style

### Characteristics
- Precise and elegant
- Often polemical
- Mathematical rigor
- Philosophical depth
- Handwritten manuscripts

### Recurring Themes
- Programming as mathematics
- Elegance essential
- Formal methods necessary
- Clear thinking
- Professional standards
