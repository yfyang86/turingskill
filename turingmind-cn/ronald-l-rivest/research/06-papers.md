# Ronald L. Rivest - Key Papers and Publications

## Foundational Papers

### RSA Algorithm
- **"A Method for Obtaining Digital Signatures and Public-Key Cryptosystems"** (1978)
  - With Adi Shamir and Leonard Adleman
  - Communications of the ACM
  - Most famous cryptography paper
  - Thousands of citations

### Hash Functions
- **"The MD5 Message-Digest Algorithm"** (RFC 1321, 1992)
  - Internet standard
  - Widely implemented
  - Later cryptanalyzed

- **"The RC5 Encryption Algorithm"** (1995)
  - Block cipher design
  - Simple and efficient

## Textbooks

### "Introduction to Algorithms" (CLRS)
- **First Edition:** 1990
- **Subsequent Editions:** 2001, 2009, 2022
- **Co-authors:** Cormen, Leiserson, Stein
- **Impact:** Standard graduate text
- **Translations:** Multiple languages
