# Kenneth E. Iverson: Writings and Communication Style

## Notation-Centered
Iverson's writing:
- Heavy use of notation
- Concise expressions
- Examples demonstrating power
- Minimal prose explanation

## Teaching Focus
- Pedagogical examples
- Progressive complexity
- Clear definitions
- Practical applications

## Turing Lecture Style
"Notation as a Tool of Thought":
- Philosophical argument
- Historical examples
- APL demonstrations
- Vision for programming
