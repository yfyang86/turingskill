# Kenneth E. Iverson: Key Decisions

## 1. Pursuing Mathematics
Farm to Harvard via military service.

## 2. Creating Notation (1957-1962)
Developed Iverson notation for teaching.

## 3. Joining IBM (1960)
Industrial research opportunity.

## 4. APL Implementation (1960s)
Made notation executable with Falkoff.

## 5. IBM Fellowship (1970)
Recognition and freedom.

## 6. Leaving IBM (1980)
Independence with IP Sharp.

## 7. Creating J (1990s)
ASCII evolution with Hui.

## 8. Open Source J
Freely distributing language.

## Pattern
Iverson consistently:
- Followed notation vision
- Prioritized teaching
- Maintained persistence
- Evolved ideas over decades
