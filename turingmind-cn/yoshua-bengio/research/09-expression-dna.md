- Deep architectures learn hierarchical abstractions
- Unsupervised learning is essential

- Compositionality is key
- System 1 and System 2 both needed
- Understanding requires more than pattern matching

### Responsible Development

- Safety and ethics are integral
- Long-term consequences matter
