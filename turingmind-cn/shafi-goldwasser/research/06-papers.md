# Shafi Goldwasser: Key Publications

## Foundational Papers

### "The Knowledge Complexity of Interactive Proof Systems" (1985)
**Authors**: S. Goldwasser, S. Micali, C. Rackoff
**Venue**: STOC 1985
**Significance**: Introduced zero-knowledge proofs. Created the field.

### "Probabilistic Encryption" (1984)
**Authors**: S. Goldwasser, S. Micali
**Venue**: Journal of Computer and System Sciences
**Significance**: Defined semantic security. Foundation for modern encryption.

### "How to Construct Random Functions" (1986)
**Authors**: O. Goldreich, S. Goldwasser, S. Micali
**Venue**: Journal of the ACM
**Significance**: Pseudo-random functions. Fundamental primitive.

## Key Results

### "How to Play Any Mental Game" (1987)
**Authors**: O. Goldreich, S. Micali, A. Wigderson
**Venue**: STOC 1987
**Significance**: Secure multi-party computation.

### "Completeness Theorems for Non-Cryptographic Fault-Tolerant Distributed Computation"
**Authors**: Same as above
**Significance**: Fundamental MPC results.

## Books and Surveys
