# Yann LeCun - Key Papers and Publications

## Foundational Papers

### "Backpropagation Applied to Handwritten Zip Code Recognition" (1989)
- **Authors:** Y. LeCun, B. Boser, J.S. Denker, D. Henderson, R.E. Howard, W. Hubbard, L.D. Jackel
- **Venue:** Neural Computation
- **Significance:** Introduced practical CNN (LeNet-1)
- **Impact:** First successful convolutional network
- **Citations:** 15,000+

### "Gradient-Based Learning Applied to Document Recognition" (1998)
- **Authors:** Y. LeCun, L. Bottou, Y. Bengio, P. Haffner
- **Venue:** Proceedings of the IEEE
- **Significance:** Definitive LeNet-5 paper
- **Content:** Complete CNN theory and practice
- **Citations:** 40,000+ (one of most cited ML papers)

### "Deep Learning" (2015)
- **Authors:** Y. LeCun, Y. Bengio, G. Hinton
- **Venue:** Nature
- **Significance:** Turing Award authors' survey
- **Content:** Comprehensive deep learning overview
- **Citations:** 30,000+

## Computer Vision Breakthroughs

### "OverFeat: Integrated Recognition, Localization and Detection" (2014)
- **Authors:** P. Sermanet, D. Eigen, X. Zhang, M. Mathieu, R. Fergus, Y. LeCun
- **Venue:** ICLR
- **Significance:** Unified CNN for multiple vision tasks

### "Rich Feature Hierarchies for Accurate Object Detection" (R-CNN) (2014)
- **Authors:** R. Girshick, J. Donahue, T. Darrell, J. Malik
- **Note:** With LeCun's student Ross Girshick at FAIR
- **Impact:** Revolutionized object detection

### "A Unified Architecture for Natural Language Processing" (2008)
- **Authors:** R. Collobert, J. Weston, L. Bottou, M. Karlen, K. Kavukcuoglu, P. Kuksa
- **Venue:** ICML
- **Significance:** Neural networks for NLP pre-transformers

## Energy-Based Models

### "A Tutorial on Energy-Based Learning" (2006)
- **Author:** Y. LeCun
- **Venue:** Predicting Structured Data, MIT Press
- **Content:** Theoretical foundations of EBMs
- **Impact:** Framework for modern self-supervised learning

### "Dimensionality Reduction by Learning an Invariant Mapping" (2006)
- **Authors:** R. Hadsell, S. Chopra, Y. LeCun
- **Venue:** CVPR
- **Significance:** Siamese networks, contrastive learning

## Optimal Brain Damage

### "Optimal Brain Damage" (1990)
- **Authors:** Y. LeCun, J.S. Denker, S.A. Solla
- **Venue:** NIPS/NeurIPS
- **Significance:** Network pruning techniques
- **Impact:** Modern neural network compression

## Capsule Networks

### "Dynamic Routing Between Capsules" (2017)
- **Authors:** S. Sabour, N. Frosst, G.E. Hinton
- **Note:** Hinton building on LeCun's CNN work
- **Venue:** NIPS/NeurIPS

### "Matrix Capsules with EM Routing" (2018)
- **Authors:** G.E. Hinton, S. Sabour, N. Frosst
- **Connection:** LeCun's influence on capsule research
