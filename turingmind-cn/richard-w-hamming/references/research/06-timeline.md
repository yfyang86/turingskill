| Year | Event |

| Year | Event |
|------|-------|
| 1946 | Joined Bell Telephone Laboratories |

| 1950 | Published "Error Detecting and Error Correcting Codes" |
| 1962 | Published "Numerical Methods for Scientists and Engineers" |
| 1968 | **Receives ACM Turing Award** |
| 1977 | Published "Digital Filters" (first edition) |
| 1976 | Left Bell Labs after 30 years |
