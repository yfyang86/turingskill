# Alan J. Perlis - External Perspectives

- "Perlis combined mathematical rigor with engineering pragmatism"
- Praised his ability to bridge theory and practice
- Valued his contributions to CMU's interdisciplinary culture

### Allen Newell (AI Pioneer)
- "He essentially created the field of programming languages"
- Admired his leadership in establishing CS at CMU
- Appreciated his wit and intellectual breadth

### John Backus (FORTRAN Creator)
- Respected his ALGOL committee leadership
- Collaborated on programming language standards
- Acknowledged his compiler construction contributions
