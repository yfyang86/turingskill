# Edsger W. Dijkstra - Key Decisions

## Career Decisions

### Mathematisch Centrum (1952)
- Chose research over industry
- Mathematical approach to computing
- ALGOL implementation work
- Foundation of career

### Electrologica and ALGOL (1956-1960)
- Implemented ALGOL 60
- Hardware design involvement
- Language standardization
- International recognition

### Eindhoven Professorship (1962)
- Academic career commitment
- Built Dutch computer science
- Research freedom
- Educational mission

### Burroughs Fellowship (1963-1973)
- Part-time industry connection
- Maintained academic independence
- Practical OS work (THE)
- Bridge theory-practice

### Move to Austin (1984)
- Left Netherlands for Texas
- Schlumberger Centennial Chair
- Continued research and teaching
- International influence

## Technical Decisions

### Shortest Path Algorithm (1956)
- Elegant solution in 20 minutes
- Published 1959
- Universal adoption
- Classic CS contribution

### Semaphores (1965)
- Synchronization primitive design
- P() and V() operations
- Solution to concurrency
- Universal adoption

### Structured Programming (1968)
- GOTO critique published
- Sparked major controversy
- Changed programming practice
- Eventually widely accepted

### Guarded Commands (1975)
- Language construct design
- Nondeterminism handling
- Influenced Ada, CSP
- Alternative to traditional conditionals
