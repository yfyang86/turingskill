# Leslie Lamport: External Perspectives

## Peer Recognition

Fred Schneider:
> "Leslie's work on logical clocks and Paxos defined distributed systems. His clarity of thought is unmatched."

Butler Lampson:

## Historical Assessments

**Distributed systems textbooks**:
Lamport's logical clocks paper is always the first or second paper assigned.

**Formal methods community**:
TLA+ brought formal specification to industry, achieving what many thought impossible.
