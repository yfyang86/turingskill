# Leslie Lamport: Selected Writings

## Books

### "LaTeX: A Document Preparation System" (1986)
**Publisher**: Addison-Wesley
**Content**: Complete guide to LaTeX
**Impact**: Standard reference for millions

### "Specifying Systems: The TLA+ Language and Tools for Hardware and Software Engineers" (2002)
**Publisher**: Addison-Wesley
**Content**: TLA+ specification language
**Impact**: Enabled industrial formal methods

### "Latex Beginner's Guide" (various editions)
Documentation for LaTeX users.

## Turing Award Lecture

### "The Computer Science of Concurrency: The Early Years"
**Venue**: ACM Turing Award Lecture, 2014
**Content**: Historical perspective on concurrent systems research.
