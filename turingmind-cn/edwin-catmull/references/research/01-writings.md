# Edwin Catmull - Key Writings and Publications

## Foundational Papers

### "A Subdivision Algorithm for Computer Display of Curved Surves" (1974)
- **Publication:** Ph.D. Thesis, University of Utah
- **Significance:** Catmull-Clark algorithm
- **Impact:** Standard surface modeling technique
- **Citations:** Thousands of follow-up works

### "Computer Display of Curved Surfaces" (1975)
- **Conference:** IEEE Conference on Computer Graphics
- **Content:** Texture mapping, surface rendering
- **Innovation:** First practical texture mapping

### "The Reyes Image Rendering Architecture" (1987)
- **Co-authors:** Robert L. Cook, Loren Carpenter
- **Conference:** ACM SIGGRAPH
- **Significance:** Foundation of RenderMan
- **Innovation:** Micropolygon rendering
