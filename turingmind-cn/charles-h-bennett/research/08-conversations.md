# Charles H. Bennett: Interviews and Dialogues

## On Meeting Brassard

> "I met Gilles swimming in Puerto Rico in 1979. I proposed quantum money, and that began our 40-year collaboration."

## On BB84

> "BB84 uses quantum mechanics to guarantee security. Any eavesdropping disturbs the quantum state, revealing the intrusion."

## On Quantum Information

> "Information is physical. This insight from Rolf Landauer shaped my entire approach to computation."

## on Quantum Teleportation

> "Quantum teleportation transfers quantum states using entanglement and classical communication. It's not science fiction."

## On IBM Research

> "IBM gave me the freedom to pursue fundamental questions for over 50 years. That's rare and valuable."

## On Reversibility

> "My 1973 paper showed computation need not dissipate energy. This connected thermodynamics and computation."

## Advice to Researchers

> "Follow fundamental questions. The applications often follow, sometimes decades later."
