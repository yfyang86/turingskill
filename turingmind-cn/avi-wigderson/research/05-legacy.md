# Avi Wigderson: Legacy and Impact

## Randomness and Computation

Wigderson's most significant legacy is reshaping understanding of randomness in computation.

### From Randomness to Hardness
Before Wigderson's work:
- Randomness was believed to provide computational power
- Derandomization seemed difficult or impossible
- No clear connection between hardness and randomness

After Wigderson's work:
- Hardness implies derandomization
- Under reasonable assumptions, P = BPP
- Randomness is either extremely powerful or unnecessary

### Paradigm Shift
The hardness-randomness connection is now a central paradigm in theoretical computer science.
