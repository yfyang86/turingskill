# Avi Wigderson - Awards and Honors

## Turing Award (2023)
- **Award**: ACM A.M. Turing Award
- **Citation**: "For reshaping our understanding of the role of randomness in computation"
- **Significance**: Highest honor in computing
- **Monetary Award**: $1 million
- **Recognition**: Computational complexity contributions

### Turing Award Lecture
- Various venues
- Randomness and computation
- Complexity theory

## Abel Prize (2021)
- **Shared with**: László Lovász
- **Citation**: For contributions to discrete mathematics and theoretical computer science
- **Significance**: Mathematics Nobel equivalent
- **Monetary Award**: 7.5 million Norwegian kroner (shared)
- **Historical**: First Abel Prize for theoretical computer science

### Abel Prize Significance
- Elevated theoretical CS in mathematics
- Recognition of CS-mathematics connection
- Historical milestone
