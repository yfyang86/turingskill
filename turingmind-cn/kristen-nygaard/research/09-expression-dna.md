# Kristen Nygaard - Expression DNA

## Communication Profile

Nygaard's expression characterized by:
- **Charismatic engagement:** Enthusiastic and inclusive
- **Political awareness:** Social context always present
- **Accessible technicality:** Complex ideas made clear
- **Activist passion:** Call to action embedded

## Linguistic Patterns

### Vocabulary Preferences
- **Democracy, participation:** Political core
- **Users, workers:** Human focus
- **Objects, classes:** Technical concepts
- **Together, we:** Collaborative emphasis

### Syntactic Structures
- "We need to..." (call to action)
- "From a user perspective..." (viewpoint shift)
- "The social implications..." (context widening)
- "Together we can..." (collective agency)

## Argumentation Style

Nygaard structures arguments:
1. **Problem identification:** Social or technical
2. **Stakeholder analysis:** Who affected
3. **Principles invoked:** Democracy, justice
4. **Proposed solution:** Technical and social
5. **Call to action:** Engagement invited
