# Patrick Hanrahan - Awards and Honors

## Turing Award (2019)
- **Award**: ACM A.M. Turing Award
- **Shared with**: Edwin Catmull
- **Citation**: "For fundamental contributions to computer graphics, and the impact of computer-generated imagery (CGI) in filmmaking and related applications"
- **Significance**: Highest honor in computing, "Nobel Prize of Computing"
- **Award Amount**: $1 million (shared)
