# Marvin Minsky - Key Collaborators

## AI Founding Circle

### Dartmouth Conference Co-Organizers (1956)
- **John McCarthy:** Co-founder of AI field
  - MIT AI Lab co-founder
  - LISP creator
  - Long-term collaborator and colleague
  - Both at Stanford and MIT

- **Claude Shannon:** Information theory pioneer
  - Dartmouth Conference participant
  - Mutual respect and influence
  - Bell Labs connection

- **Nathaniel Rochester:** IBM 701 designer
  - Dartmouth Conference co-organizer
  - Early AI supporter

## MIT AI Laboratory

### Co-Founders and Early Leaders
- **Seymour Papert:** Co-founder, Logo creator
  - "Perceptrons" co-author
  - Constructionism in education
  - Long-term collaborator until disagreements

- **John McCarthy:** Co-founder (until move to Stanford)
  - Shaped initial AI Lab direction
  - LISP and AI foundations

- **Ray Kurzweil:** Futurist, inventor (student)

- **Patrick Winston:** AI Lab Director successor
- **Danny Hillis:** Connection Machine (PhD 1981)

- Hundreds of influential AI researchers
