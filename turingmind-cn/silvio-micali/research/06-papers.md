# Silvio Micali: Key Publications

## Foundational Papers

### "The Knowledge Complexity of Interactive Proof Systems" (1985)
**Authors**: S. Goldwasser, S. Micali, C. Rackoff
**Venue**: STOC 1985
**Significance**: Zero-knowledge proofs invented.

### "Probabilistic Encryption" (1984)
**Authors**: S. Goldwasser, S. Micali
**Venue**: JCSS 1984
**Significance**: Semantic security defined.

### "How to Construct Random Functions" (1986)
**Authors**: O. Goldreich, S. Goldwasser, S. Micali
**Venue**: JACM 1986
**Significance**: Pseudo-random functions.

### "How to Play Any Mental Game" (1987)
**Authors**: O. Goldreich, S. Micali, A. Wigderson
**Venue**: STOC 1987
**Significance**: Secure multi-party computation.
