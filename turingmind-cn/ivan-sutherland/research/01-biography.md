# Ivan Sutherland - Biography

## Early Life (1938-1959)

**Birth and Education**
- Born May 16, 1938, in Hastings, Nebraska
- Father: Ivan Sutherland, civil engineer
- Mother: Myrtle Sutherland
- Grew up in various locations (father's career)

**Scarsdale High School**
- Attended in Scarsdale, New York
- Strong interest in electrical engineering
- Built electronic devices
- Excellent science and math student

**Carnegie Institute of Technology (1955-1959)**
- B.S. in Electrical Engineering (1959)
- Electrical engineering focus
- Early computer exposure
- Distinguished undergraduate

## Graduate Studies and Sketchpad

**California Institute of Technology (1959-1960)**
- M.S. in Electrical Engineering (1960)
- Continued interest in computers
- Transition to MIT for PhD

**MIT Lincoln Laboratory (1960-1963)**
- PhD work at MIT
- Lincoln Laboratory TX-2 computer access
- Sketchpad development (1961-1963)
- Revolutionary interactive graphics

**Sketchpad (1963)**
- PhD dissertation: "Sketchpad: A Man-Machine Graphical Communication System"
- First interactive computer graphics system
- Constraint-based drawing
- Light pen input
- Direct manipulation interface

## Career Highlights

**Harvard University (1965-1966)**
- Associate Professor
- Early tenure track
- Continued graphics research
- Brief academic period

**University of Utah (1966-1976)**
- Professor of Computer Science
- Founded computer graphics research group
- EVANS & SUTHERLAND co-founded (1968)
- Recruited future graphics pioneers

**Caltech (1975-1980)**
- Fletcher Jones Professor
- Computer Science Department Head
- VLSI design research
- Mead-Conway collaboration

**Sun Microsystems (1990-2009)**
- Fellow and Vice President
- SPARC architecture involvement
- Asynchronous computing research
- Industrial research application

## Major Innovations

**Sketchpad (1963)**
- Interactive computer graphics birth

**Asynchronous Computing (1990s-2000s)**
- Self-timed circuits research
- Clockless computing
