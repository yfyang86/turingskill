# Robert W. Floyd - External Perspectives

## Colleague Perspectives

### Stanford Colleagues
- **Donald Knuth:** "Bob Floyd had the most elegant mind in computer science. Every algorithm he touched became beautiful. His friendship meant a great deal to me."
- **Gene Golub:** "A brilliant mathematician who happened to work in computer science. Warm, generous colleague."
- **John McCarthy:** "His verification work transformed how we think about program correctness."

### Carnegie Mellon Colleagues
- **Alan Perlis:** "I hired Bob despite no PhD because his brilliance was obvious. One of my best decisions. He became a cornerstone of our department."
- **Various CMU faculty:** Early supporter and brilliant contributor
