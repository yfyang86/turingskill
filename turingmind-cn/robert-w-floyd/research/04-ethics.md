# Robert W. Floyd - Ethics and Philosophy

## Research Philosophy

### Elegance in Science
- Sought elegant solutions
- Beauty in algorithms
- Simple yet powerful
- "The best algorithms are beautiful"

### Theory and Practice
- Bridged both worlds
- Theory guided by practice
- Practical algorithms with theoretical foundation
- No false dichotomy

### Collaboration Ethics
