# Robert W. Floyd - Key Papers and Publications

## Foundational Papers

### Program Verification
- **"Assigning Meanings to Programs"** (1967)
  - Proceedings of Symposium in Applied Mathematics, Vol. 19
  - Mathematical Aspects of Computer Science
  - Most influential paper
  - Foundation of program verification
  - Inductive assertions method
  - Pre/post conditions

### Algorithms
- **"Algorithm 245: Treesort 3 [M1]"** (1964)
  - Communications of the ACM, Vol. 7, No. 12
  - HeapSort algorithm published
  - In-place sorting
  - Tree-based heap structure

- **"Algorithm 97: Shortest Path"** (1962)
  - Communications of the ACM, Vol. 5, No. 6
  - Floyd's all-pairs shortest path
  - Dynamic programming approach

### Parsing and Languages
- **"Syntactic Analysis and Operator Precedence"** (1963)
  - Journal of the ACM, Vol. 10, No. 3
  - Operator precedence grammars
  - Efficient parsing methods
  - Compiler construction

- **"Bounded-Context Syntactic Analysis"** (1964)
  - Communications of the ACM
  - Parsing theory contributions
