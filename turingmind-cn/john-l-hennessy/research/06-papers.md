# John L. Hennessy: Key Papers and Publications

## Foundational RISC Papers

### "VLSI Processor Architecture" (1982)
Hennessy, J. L., et al. IEEE Transactions on Computers.

Described the MIPS architecture and demonstrated the feasibility of RISC approaches in VLSI technology.

### "MIPS: A Microprocessor Architecture" (1984)
Hennessy, J. L. ACM SIGARCH Computer Architecture News.

Comprehensive description of the MIPS architecture, instruction set, and design philosophy.

### "The Case for the Reduced Instruction Set Computer" (1982)
Patterson, D. A., & Ditzel, D. R. (with response by Clark and Strecker).

While not by Hennessy, this paper (and responses) defined the RISC debate that Hennessy helped lead.

## Compiler and Architecture Papers

### "Register Allocation via Coloring" (1981)
Chaitin, G. J., et al. (Hennessy as co-author on related work).

Research on compiler optimization techniques essential for RISC processors.

### "Postpass Code Optimization of Pipeline Constraints" (1982)
Hennessy, J. L., & Gross, T. R. ACM Transactions on Programming Languages and Systems.

Addressed compiler techniques for optimizing code for pipelined processors.

## Quantitative Architecture Papers

### "Cache Performance of the SPEC92 Benchmark Suite" (1993)
Various papers with Hennessy's students analyzing memory hierarchy performance.

### "Evaluating Memory Hierarchy Performance" (Various)
Research papers establishing quantitative approaches to memory system evaluation.

## The Textbook

### "Computer Architecture: A Quantitative Approach" (1990-Present)
Hennessy, J. L., & Patterson, D. A.

Six editions (1990, 1996, 2003, 2007, 2012, 2019).

The definitive computer architecture textbook, translated into multiple languages, used at universities worldwide.

### "Computer Organization and Design: The Hardware/Software Interface"
Patterson, D. A., & Hennessy, J. L.

Undergraduate-level companion text.

## Parallel Architecture Research

### "The Stanford DASH Multiprocessor" (1992)
Lenoski, D., et al. (Hennessy as co-author). IEEE Computer.

Described the Stanford Directory Architecture for Shared Memory multiprocessor.

### Various Papers on Cache Coherence and Memory Consistency
Research on parallel processor architecture and memory systems.

## Characteristics of His Research Writing

### Quantitative Focus
- Heavy use of data and measurement
- Statistical analysis of performance
- Benchmark results and comparisons

### Clarity of Explanation
- Clear technical writing
- Accessible to advanced students
- Well-organized presentation

### Rigorous Methodology
- Careful experimental design
- Reproducible results
- Honest acknowledgment of limitations

## Citation Impact

### Highly Cited Papers
Hennessy's papers are among the most cited in computer architecture:
- Architecture textbook cited thousands of times
- RISC papers continue to be referenced
- Compiler optimization papers remain relevant

### Influence on Field
His writing style and methodology have influenced:
- How architecture papers are written
- Standards for empirical evaluation
- Educational approaches in the field
