- Formal definitions with rigor

- Careful quantifier usage
- Technical term precision

**Logical Structure**
- If-then argumentation

**Understated Delivery**
- Minimal superlatives

## Key Phrases and Formulations

**Signature Statements**
- "We say a problem is NP-complete if..."
- "It follows that P = NP if and only if..."
- "The question remains open..."
- "Feasibly constructive proof..."

**Defining Formulations**
- "A set S is in NP if there exists..."
- "Polynomial-time reduction"
- "Cook reduction vs. Karp reduction"
- "Propositional proof system"

**Careful Qualifications**
- "Assuming P ≠ NP..."
- "It is widely believed that..."
- "Current techniques do not suffice..."
- "The exact complexity remains unknown..."
