# Richard W. Hamming - Writings

## Original Publications

### Books Authored
- **"Numerical Methods for Scientists and Engineers"** (1962, 1973, 1986)
  - McGraw-Hill, then Dover
  - Classic textbook, still in print
  - Translated into multiple languages
  - Covers: interpolation, integration, differential equations, linear algebra

- **"Digital Filters"** (1977, 1989, 1997)
  - Three editions
  - Prentice-Hall, then Dover
  - Standard reference for DSP

- **"The Art of Doing Science and Engineering: Learning to Learn"** (1997)
  - Gordon and Breach (now Stripe Press)
  - Based on NPS course lectures
  - Philosophical approach to research

- **"Methods of Mathematics Applied to Calculus, Probability, and Statistics"** (1985)
  - Prentice-Hall
  - Comprehensive mathematical reference

- **"Coding and Information Theory"** (1980, 1986)
  - Prentice-Hall
  - Two editions

### Foundational Papers
- **"Error Detecting and Error Correcting Codes"** (1950)
  - Bell System Technical Journal
  - Single most important paper
  - Foundation of coding theory
