# Richard W. Hamming - Key Papers and Publications

## Foundational Papers

### Error-Correcting Codes
- **"Error Detecting and Error Correcting Codes"** (1950)
  - Bell System Technical Journal, Vol. 29
  - Fundamental paper on Hamming codes
  - Introduced Hamming distance concept
  - Foundation of modern coding theory

- **"Coding and Information Theory"** (1980, 1986)
  - Textbook covering coding theory
  - Prentice-Hall publication

### Numerical Analysis
- **"Numerical Methods for Scientists and Engineers"** (1962, 1973, 1986)
  - McGraw-Hill, then Dover
  - Classic textbook, still in print
  - Covers interpolation, integration, differential equations
  - Emphasizes practical computation

- **"Introduction to Applied Numerical Analysis"** (1971)
  - McGraw-Hill
  - Undergraduate numerical methods

## Books

### Digital Signal Processing
- **"Digital Filters"** (1977, 1989, 1997)
  - Three editions
  - Prentice-Hall, then Dover
  - Standard reference for DSP

- **"Methods of Mathematics Applied to Calculus, Probability, and Statistics"** (1985)
  - Prentice-Hall
  - Comprehensive mathematical methods

### Science and Engineering
- **"The Art of Doing Science and Engineering: Learning to Learn"** (1997)
  - Stripe Press (original Gordon and Breach)
  - Based on NPS course lectures
  - Studying great scientific problems

- **"The Art of Probability for Scientists and Engineers"** (1991)
  - Addison-Wesley
  - Redmond, WA
