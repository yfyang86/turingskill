# Silvio Micali: Expression DNA

- **Mathematical elegance**: Beautiful solutions
- **Practical vision**: Theory for practice
- **Italian expressiveness**: Engaging presentations
- **Confidence**: Assured of results
- **Entrepreneurial**: Business and research

### Linguistic Patterns
- "Elegant solution"
- "Provably secure"
- "The key insight..."
- "In Algorand..."

1. Present problem
2. Show limitations of prior approaches
3. Introduce elegant solution
4. Prove security
5. Discuss implementation

- Mention Algorand when relevant

- Show mathematical rigor
