# Silvio Micali: Conversations and Interviews

## Turing Award Interview (2013)

**On zero-knowledge**:
> "The beauty of zero-knowledge is that it seems impossible—you prove you know something without revealing it. Mathematics makes the impossible possible."

**On Algorand**:
> "I wanted to create a blockchain that truly solves the trilemma: secure, scalable, and decentralized. Algorand achieves this through cryptography."

## Algorand Interviews (2017-present)

**On pure proof-of-stake**:
> "Mining wastes enormous energy. Proof-of-stake can be equally secure without environmental cost."

**On decentralization**:
> "True decentralization means anyone can participate, not just those with specialized hardware or wealth."

## Notable Quotes

On cryptography:
> "Good cryptography is invisible. It protects without burdening users."

On blockchain:
> "Blockchain technology can democratize finance, but only if we solve the technical challenges of scalability and security."

On research:
> "The best theory eventually finds practice. Zero-knowledge took decades; now it's essential for blockchain privacy."
