# Edwin Catmull - Timeline

## 1940s-1960s: Early Life and Education

### 1945
- **March 31:** Born in Parkersburg, West Virginia

### 1950s
- **Grew up:** Salt Lake City, Utah
- **Dream:** Disney animator

### 1960s
- **University of Utah enrollment**
- **Studies:** Physics and computer science

## 1970s: Graduate Studies and Early Career

### 1970
- **Bachelor's:** Physics and CS

### 1972
- **First CGI:** Hand and face animation

### 1974
- **Ph.D.:** Computer Science, Utah
- **Thesis:** Subdivision surfaces
- **Joined Lucasfilm**

### 1979
- **Recruited by George Lucas**
- **Role:** Build graphics group

## 1980s: The RenderMan Era

### 1980
- **Digital paint system**

### 1982
- **Genesis sequence:** Star Trek II

### 1984
- **André and Wally B.**

### 1986
- **February 3:** **Pixar founded**
- **Steve Jobs acquisition**
- **CTO role**

### 1988
- **Tin Toy:** Academy Award
- **RenderMan commercial release**

## 1990s: Feature Animation Revolution

### 1991
- **Disney deal:** Three-picture agreement

### 1995
- **November 22:** **Toy Story released**
- **First CGI feature**
- **Pixar IPO**

### 2001
- **Promoted:** President of Pixar

### 2004
- **The Incredibles**

## 2000s: Disney Era

### 2006
- **January 24:** **Disney acquires Pixar**
- **$7.4 billion**
- **President:** Disney and Pixar Animation

### 2009
- **Up:** Second Best Picture nom
- **Gordon E. Sawyer Award**

### 2014
- **Jean Hersholt Award**
- **Creativity, Inc. published**

### 2016
- **Stepped down announcement**

### 2018
- **October 23:** **Retired from Disney**

### 2019
- **March 18:** **Turing Award announced**
- **With:** Pat Hanrahan
- **Official retirement**

## 2020s: Post-Retirement

### 2020-Present

- **Education focus**
- **Mentoring**
