# Edwin Catmull - Key Collaborators

## Founding Partners

### Steve Jobs (1955-2011)
- Acquired Pixar from Lucasfilm in 1986
- Financial backing and strategic vision
- Guided Pixar through IPO and growth
- Instrumental in Disney merger negotiations

### John Lasseter
- Chief Creative Officer at Pixar
- Directed Toy Story, Cars, and other classics
- Co-architect of Pixar's creative culture
- Collaborated on storytelling-technology integration

## Disney Collaboration

### Bob Iger
- CEO of Disney during Pixar acquisition
- Negotiated $7.4 billion acquisition in 2006
- Preserved Pixar's independent culture

### Roy Disney
- Championed Disney-Pixar relationship
- Advocated for creative autonomy

## Technical Collaborators

### Pat Hanrahan
- Pixar founding employee
- Led RenderMan development
- Shared 2019 Turing Award
- Stanford professor

### Alvy Ray Smith
- Co-founder of Pixar
- Pioneer in digital painting
- Developed early pixel technology

### Loren Carpenter
- SIGGRAPH pioneer
- Developed Reyes rendering
- Expert in fractal landscapes

## Academic Influences

### Ivan Sutherland
- Ph.D. advisor at University of Utah
- "Father of Computer Graphics"
- Influenced Catmull's research direction

### Martin Newell
- Utah colleague
- Created Utah teapot model
- Collaborated on rendering algorithms

### David Evans
- University of Utah professor
- Founded Evans & Sutherland
- Early computer graphics entrepreneur

## Industry Partnerships

### Industrial Light & Magic
- Early RenderMan customer
