# Edwin Catmull - Key Papers and Publications

## Foundational Dissertation

### "A Subdivision Algorithm for Computer Display of Curved Surfaces" (1974)
- **Publication:** Ph.D. Thesis, University of Utah
- **Significance:** Introduced Catmull-Clark subdivision algorithm
- **Impact:** Standard technique for smooth surface modeling
- **Citations:** Thousands of follow-up works

## Landmark Technical Papers

### "Computer Display of Curved Surfaces" (1975)
- **Conference:** IEEE Conference on Computer Graphics
- **Content:** Early texture mapping and surface rendering
- **Innovation:** First practical texture mapping algorithm

### "The Reyes Image Rendering Architecture" (1987)
- **Authors:** Robert L. Cook, Loren Carpenter, Edwin Catmull
- **Conference:** ACM SIGGRAPH
- **Significance:** Foundation of RenderMan renderer
- **Innovation:** Micropolygon rendering, motion blur, depth of field

### "A Hidden-Surface Algorithm with Anti-Aliasing" (1978)
- **Conference:** ACM SIGGRAPH
- **Content:** Z-buffer improvements with anti-aliasing
- **Impact:** Enhanced image quality in early CGI
