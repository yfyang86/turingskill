# Edwin Catmull - Legacy and Impact

## Revolutionizing Animation

### End of Traditional Animation Dominance
- Proved computer animation could match hand-drawn artistry
- Toy Story (1995) marked paradigm shift
- Disney eventually closed hand-drawn animation division
- Digital animation became industry standard

### Technical Standards
- RenderMan became industry rendering standard
- Influenced GPU architecture development
- Established computer graphics as art form

## Pixar's Creative Legacy

### Filmography Impact
- 24 feature films, virtually all critical successes
- 14 Academy Awards for Best Animated Feature
- Cumulative box office exceeding $14 billion
- "Pixar formula" for storytelling emulated industry-wide

### Creative Culture Model
- "Pixar way" studied by businesses worldwide
- Creativity, Inc. became management bestseller
- Braintrust model adopted by other companies
- Influenced Silicon Valley startup culture

## Industry Transformation

### Visual Effects
- RenderMan used in landmark films
- Jurassic Park proved CGI viability for live action
- Enabled modern superhero and sci-fi films
- Transformed Hollywood production pipelines

### Technology Transfer
- GPU design influenced by graphics research
- Video game graphics advancement
- Virtual reality rendering techniques
- Medical and scientific visualization

## Academic Contributions

### University of Utah Legacy
- Part of "Utah mafia" of graphics pioneers
- Trained generation of researchers
- Established computer graphics curriculum
- Influenced graphics education worldwide

### Research Community
- SIGGRAPH leadership and participation
- Mentored dozens of graphics researchers
- Bridged academic and industrial research
- Published foundational papers
