# Charles H. Bennett - Biography

## Overview
Charles Henry Bennett (born 1943) is an American physicist and computer scientist at IBM Research. He shared the 2025 ACM A.M. Turing Award with Gilles Brassard for their foundational work on quantum cryptography and quantum information.

## Early Life
- **Born**: 1943 in New York City, USA
- **Family**: Middle-class background
- **Grew up**: Westchester County, New York
- **Early Interests**: Chemistry, physics, computers

## Education
- **BA** (1964): Brandeis University, Chemistry
- **PhD** (1970): Harvard University, Molecular Dynamics
- **Postdoc** (1970-1972): University of Warsaw (physics)
- **PhD Advisor**: Unknown (Chemistry focus)

## Career

### IBM Research (1972-present)
- **Joined**: Thomas J. Watson Research Center
- **Location**: Yorktown Heights, New York
- **Focus**: Physics of computation
- **Quantum information**: Pioneer

## Major Contributions
