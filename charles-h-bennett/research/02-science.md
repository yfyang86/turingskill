## Quantum Cryptography

### BB84 Protocol (1984)
- **With Brassard**: First QKD
- **Mechanism**: Quantum states for key
- **Security**: Physics-based
- **Eavesdropping**: Detectable

### Protocol Details
- **States**: Non-orthogonal qubits
- **Bases**: Rectilinear and diagonal
- **Security**: No-cloning theorem
- **Key rate**: Fraction of bits

### Impact
- **Field creation**: Quantum cryptography
- **Commercialization**: Quantum systems
- **Satellite QKD**: Long-distance
- **Security**: Information-theoretic
