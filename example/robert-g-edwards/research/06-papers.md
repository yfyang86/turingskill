# 06 - 核心论文与参考文献

## Edwards 核心论文

1. **Edwards RG.** Maturation in vitro of human ovarian oocytes. *Lancet* 1965; 2:926-929.
   - 人类卵母细胞体外成熟的首次报告

2. **Edwards RG, Bavister BD, Steptoe PC.** Early stages of fertilization in vitro of human oocytes matured in vitro. *Nature* 1969; 221:632-635.
   - 人类卵子体外受精的早期阶段

3. **Edwards RG, Steptoe PC, Purdy JM.** Fertilization and cleavage in vitro of preovulator human oocytes. *Nature* 1970; 227:1307-1309.
   - 体外受精卵的卵裂观察

4. **Steptoe PC, Edwards RG.** Birth after the reimplantation of a human embryo. *Lancet* 1978; 2:366.
   - 世界第一例试管婴儿的诞生报告

5. **Edwards RG, Steptoe PC.** A matter of life: the story of in vitro fertilization. London: Hutchinson, 1980.
   - Edwards与Steptoe合著的IVF自述
