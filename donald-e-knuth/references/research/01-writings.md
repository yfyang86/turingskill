## Other Technical Books

### Concrete Mathematics (1989)
- **Authors**: Ronald L. Graham, Donald E. Knuth, Oren Patashnik
- **Publisher**: Addison-Wesley
- **Content**: Foundation for analysis of algorithms; recurrence relations, sums, number theory

### Literate Programming (1992)
- **Publisher**: CSLI Publications
- **Content**: Essays on literate programming methodology; WEB system

### The Stanford GraphBase (1993)
- **Publisher**: ACM Press
- **Content**: Platform for combinatorial computing; benchmark problems

### MMIXware (1999)
- **Publisher**: Springer
- **Content**: Complete MMIX architecture specification

## Non-Technical Works

### Surreal Numbers (1974)
- **Format**: Mathematical novelette
- **Content**: Introduction to John Conway's surreal numbers
- **Style**: Dialog narrative form

### 3:16 Bible Texts Illuminated (1991)
- **Publisher**: A-R Editions
- **Content**: Calligraphic meditations on Bible verses
- **Pages**: 268

### Things a Computer Scientist Rarely Talks About (2001)
- **Publisher**: CSLI Publications
- **Content**: Lectures on theology and computer science

## Early Publications

### The Art of Computer Programming (Original Plan, 1962)
- Initially contracted as single compiler book
- Expanded to 12 chapters, then reorganized to 7 volumes
- First draft completed while at Caltech

### Algorithmic Papers (1957-1970)
- Early work on programming language design
- Papers on compiler construction
- IBM 650 assembler improvements (unpublished, student work)
