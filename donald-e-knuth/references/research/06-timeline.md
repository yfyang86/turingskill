# Donald E. Knuth - Detailed Chronology

## Early Life and Education (1938-1963)

| Year | Age | Event |
|------|-----|-------|
| 1938 | 0 | Born January 10, Milwaukee, Wisconsin |
| 1950 | 12 | Won Ziegler candy contest (4,500+ words) |
| 1956 | 18 | Graduated high school (highest GPA in school history) |
| 1956 | 18 | Entered Case Institute of Technology (physics major) |
| 1957 | 19 | "Potrzebie System" published in MAD Magazine |
| 1958 | 20 | Created basketball statistics program (CBS News feature) |
| 1958 | 20 | Switched major to mathematics |
| 1960 | 22 | BS and MS in Mathematics (simultaneous award) |
| 1960 | 22 | Entered Caltech graduate school |
| 1962 | 24 | Approached by Addison-Wesley for compiler book |
| 1962 | 24 | Began writing TAOCP |
| 1963 | 25 | PhD in Mathematics (thesis: Finite Semifields) |
| 1963 | 25 | Joined Caltech faculty as Assistant Professor |

## Early Career and TAOCP Launch (1963-1974)

| Year | Age | Event |
|------|-----|-------|
| 1963 | 25 | Assistant Professor, Caltech |
| 1965 | 27 | Associate Professor, Caltech |
| 1965 | 27 | Joined American Guild of Organists |
| 1967 | 29 | TAOCP manuscript expanding beyond single book |
| 1968 | 30 | **Volume 1: Fundamental Algorithms published** |
| 1968 | 30 | Professor of Computer Science, Stanford University |
| 1969 | 31 | **Volume 2: Seminumerical Algorithms published** |
| 1971 | 33 | First ACM Grace Murray Hopper Award recipient |
| 1973 | 35 | **Volume 3: Sorting and Searching published** |
| 1973 | 35 | Elected to National Academy of Sciences |
| 1974 | 36 | **ACM Turing Award** (youngest recipient) |

## TeX Development Years (1974-1990)

| Year | Age | Event |
|------|-----|-------|
| 1974 | 36 | "Structured Programming with go to Statements" published |
| 1977 | 39 | Disappointed with Volume 2 second edition typesetting |
| 1977 | 39 | **Began TeX development** |
| 1978 | 40 | First TeX version (SAIL language) |
| 1978 | 40 | Gibbs Lecture: "Mathematical Typography" (AMS) |
| 1979 | 41 | **National Medal of Science** |
| 1980 | 42 | TeX82 development began (WEB/Pascal) |
| 1982 | 44 | TeX82 released; version number system begun |
| 1984 | 46 | **The TeXbook published** |
| 1984 | 46 | Literate Programming concept introduced |
| 1986 | 48 | Computers & Typesetting volumes A-E published |
| 1989 | 51 | **TeX 3.0 released** (π convergence begins) |
| 1990 | 52 | TeX declared stable (bug fixes only) |
