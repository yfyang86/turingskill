# Gilles Brassard: Key Collaborators

## Charles H. Bennett

### Relationship
Co-recipient of the 2025 Turing Award. Collaborators for over 40 years in quantum information science.

### How They Met
Met in 1979 while swimming off the coast of Puerto Rico during a theoretical computing conference. Bennett approached Brassard with an idea for quantum money.

### Joint Achievements
- BB84 quantum cryptography protocol (1984)
- Quantum teleportation (1993)
- Entanglement distillation (1996)
- Foundational quantum information science

## Université de Montréal Colleagues

### Students and Postdocs
Brassard has trained generations of quantum information researchers at the University of Montreal.

## CIFAR Collaborators

Brassard has been associated with CIFAR's Quantum Information Science program since 2002.

## John E. Hopcroft

### Relationship
Brassard's PhD advisor at Cornell University (Hopcroft won Turing Award in 1986).

## International Collaborations

Brassard has worked with researchers worldwide on quantum cryptography and information theory.

- Long-term sustained partnerships
- Interdisciplinary bridging
