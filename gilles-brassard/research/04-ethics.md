# Gilles Brassard: Ethics and Responsibility

## Open Scientific Communication

Brassard's research on quantum cryptography has been openly published:
- BB84 protocol freely available
- Quantum teleportation openly shared
- Educational contributions through teaching

## Dual-Use Considerations

Quantum cryptography has both beneficial and concerning applications:
- **Positive**: Unbreakable security for communications
- **Concerning**: Could enable unbreakable encryption for criminals

### Brassard's Position
Focuses on fundamental science and education, leaving policy to society.

## Academic Integrity

- Proper attribution in publications
- Generous credit to collaborators
- Honest about assumptions and limitations

## International Collaboration

Brassard has collaborated with researchers worldwide, including those from competing nations, reflecting belief in science as universal human endeavor.

## Overall Assessment

Brassard demonstrates commitment to:
- Open scientific communication
- International collaboration
- Fundamental research
- Education and mentorship

No major ethical controversies mark his career.
