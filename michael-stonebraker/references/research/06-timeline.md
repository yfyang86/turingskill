# Michael Stonebraker - Timeline

## 1940s-1960s: Early Life and Education

### 1943
- **October 11:** Born in Newburyport, Massachusetts

### 1965
- **Bachelor's degree:** Princeton University

### 1966
- **Master's degree:** University of Michigan

### 1971
- **Ph.D.:** University of Michigan
- **Thesis:** Database management systems

## 1971-1996: Berkeley Era

### 1971
- **Joined University of California, Berkeley**
- **Position:** Assistant Professor of Computer Science

### 1973
- **INGRES project begins**
- **Goal:** Relational database implementation

### 1974
- **INGRES prototype operational**
- **Team:** Graduate students including Jerry Held, Gene Wong

### 1976
- **INGRES paper published** (TODS)
- **Significance:** First relational system description

### 1980
- **Relational Technology, Inc. founded**
- **Commercial INGRES release**

### 1982
- **Promoted to Full Professor**

### 1984
- **Left commercial INGRES project**
- **Return to research focus**

### 1985
- **QUEL vs. SQL debate**
- **INGGRES adopts SQL**

### 1986
- **Postgres project begins**
- **Goal:** Object-relational database

### 1988
- **First Postgres prototype**
- **PostQUEL query language**

### 1992
- **Illustra Information Technologies founded**
- **Postgres commercialization**

### 1994
- **Illustra product release**

### 1996
- **Joined MIT**
- **Position:** Adjunct Professor

### 1996
- **Informix acquires Illustra**

## 1996-Present: MIT Era

### 1996
- **Berkeley departure**
- **MIT full-time appointment**

- **Stream processing database**
