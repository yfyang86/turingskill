- **Joined Stanford faculty**
- **Department:** Electrical Engineering
- **Rank:** Assistant Professor

### 1971

- **Met Whitfield Diffie:** Stanford visit

- **Outcome:** Began working together

### 1975
- **Promoted:** Full Professor

### 1976

- **Publication:** IEEE Transactions on Information Theory
- **Significance:** Public-key cryptography invented

### 1977
