# Leonard M. Adleman - Key Papers and Publications

## Foundational Papers

### RSA Algorithm
- **"A Method for Obtaining Digital Signatures and Public-Key Cryptosystems"** (1978)
  - With Rivest and Shamir
  - Communications of the ACM
  - Most cited cryptography paper
  - Foundation of public-key crypto

### DNA Computing
- **"Molecular Computation of Solutions to Combinatorial Problems"** (1994)
  - Science magazine
  - Hamiltonian path experiment
  - Founded DNA computing field
  - Widely publicized
