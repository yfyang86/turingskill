# Jack Dongarra - Career Timeline

## Argonne National Laboratory (1972-1989)

### Early Years (1972-1979)
- **Role**: Research Scientist
- **Group**: Mathematics and Computer Science Division
- **Focus**: Linear algebra software development
- **Projects**: Early LINPACK development

### PhD Completion (1980)
- **PhD**: University of New Mexico, Applied Mathematics
- **Advisor**: Cleve Moler
- **Continued at Argonne** while completing PhD

### Peak Argonne Period (1980-1989)
- **LINPACK Release**: Complete library
- **BLAS Development**: Level 1, 2, 3 specifications
- **International Recognition**: Growing reputation
- **Collaborations**: Worldwide network

## University of Tennessee (1989-present)

- **Research**: LAPACK development
- **Teaching**: Graduate courses

### Distinguished Professor (2002-present)
- **Promotion**: Distinguished Professor

- **ICL Growth**: Major research group
- **National Recognition**: Leading HPC researcher
