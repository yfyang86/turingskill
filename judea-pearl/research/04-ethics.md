# Judea Pearl: Ethics and Responsibility

## Causal Inference Ethics
Pearl's work on causal inference carries profound ethical dimensions:

### Policy and Decision-Making
Causal inference affects:
- **Medical treatments**: Who gets which treatment?
- **Social policies**: Which interventions work?
- **Economic decisions**: Policy effects on populations

Getting causality wrong has real consequences.

### Fairness and Discrimination
Causal models can:
- **Reveal bias**: Discriminatory causal pathways
- **Enable fairness**: Designing equitable policies
- **Mask injustice**: Wrong causal assumptions perpetuate bias

Pearl's work on causal discrimination analysis addresses these concerns.

- **Music for peace**: Daniel Pearl World Music Days
- **Journalism ethics**: Honoring Daniel's values

This demonstrates turning tragedy into positive action.
