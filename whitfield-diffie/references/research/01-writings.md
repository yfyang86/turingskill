# Whitfield Diffie - Key Writings and Publications

## Foundational Papers

### "New Directions in Cryptography" (1976)
- **Co-author:** Martin E. Hellman
- **Journal:** IEEE Transactions on Information Theory
- **Significance:** Introduced public-key cryptography
- **Impact:** Foundation of modern secure communications
- **Citations:** 15,000+

### "Multiuser Cryptographic Techniques" (1976)
- **Conference:** National Computer Conference
- **Content:** Early public-key concepts
- **Significance:** Conference presentation of key ideas

### "Exhaustive Cryptanalysis of the NBS Data Encryption Standard" (1977)
- **Co-author:** Martin Hellman
- **Content:** Cryptanalysis of DES
- **Impact:** Influenced cryptography policy debate

### "Privacy and Authentication: An Introduction to Cryptography" (1979)
- **Co-authors:** Martin Hellman
- **Journal:** Proceedings of the IEEE
- **Content:** Survey article for broad audience
- **Use:** Educational standard
