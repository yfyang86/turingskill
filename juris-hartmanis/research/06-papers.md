# 06 - 重要著作与论文

## 奠基性论文

### 1. "On the Computational Complexity of Algorithms" (1965)
**作者**：Juris Hartmanis, Richard E. Stearns
**期刊**：Transactions of the American Mathematical Society, Vol. 117, pp. 285-306
**贡献**：创立计算复杂性理论，定义时间/空间复杂度，证明复杂性层次定理。
**引用**：超过8,000次，理论计算机科学奠基性论文。

### 2. "Memory Bounds for Recognition of Context-Free and Context-Sensitive Languages" (1965)
**作者**：Juris Hartmanis, P.M. Lewis II, R.E. Stearns
**会议**：IEEE FOCS 1965
**贡献**：早期空间复杂性研究，形式语言与复杂性联系。

## 重要论文

### 3. "Computational Complexity of Random Access Stored Program Machines" (1968)
**作者**：Juris Hartmanis, John Hopcroft
**期刊**：Mathematical Systems Theory
**贡献**：扩展复杂性理论到更现实的计算模型。

### 4. "The Use of Lists in the Study of Undecidable Problems in Automata Theory" (1969)
**作者**：Juris Hartmanis
**期刊**：Journal of Computer and System Sciences
**贡献**：连接自动机理论与可计算性理论。

### 5. "On the Problem of Finding Natural Computational Complexity Measures" (1972)
**作者**：Juris Hartmanis, Janos Simon
**会议**：STOC 1972
**贡献**：讨论复杂性测度的公理化。

### 6. "On Polynomial Time Computable Problems" (1976)
**作者**：Juris Hartmanis
**会议**：STOC 1976
**贡献**：对多项式时间类的深入研究。

### 7. "Relations between Diagonalization, Proof Systems, and Complexity Gaps" (1979)
**作者**：Juris Hartmanis
**会议**：STOC 1979
**贡献**：连接逻辑证明系统与复杂性理论。

## 综述与专著

### 8. "Algebraic Structure Theory of Sequential Machines" (1966)
**作者**：Juris Hartmanis, R.E. Stearns
**出版社**：Prentice-Hall
**内容**：自动机理论的代数方法。

### 9. "Computational Complexity Theory" (1989)
**编者**：Juris Hartmanis (主编)
**系列**：AMS Proceedings of Symposia in Applied Mathematics
**内容**：复杂性理论综述文集。

## 自传与反思

### 10. "Observations about the Development of Theoretical Computer Science" (1981)
**期刊**：Annals of the History of Computing
**内容**：Hartmanis对理论计算机科学发展的个人观察。

### 11. "The Realm of Complexity" (1994)
**期刊**：Complexity Theory Retrospective (Springer)
**内容**：图灵奖得主的个人学术回忆录，详细记录了Hartmanis-Stearns合作的历程。

## 晚年论文

### 12. "Turing Award Lecture: On Computational Complexity and the Nature of Computer Science" (1994)
**期刊**：Communications of the ACM
**内容**：图灵奖演讲，讨论复杂性理论的意义和未来。

### 13. "Goedel, von Neumann and the P=?NP Problem" (1993)
**作者**：Juris Hartmanis
**期刊**：Bulletin of the European Association for Theoretical Computer Science
**内容**：P vs NP问题的历史追溯。
