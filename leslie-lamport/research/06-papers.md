# Leslie Lamport: Key Publications

## Foundational Papers

### "Time, Clocks, and the Ordering of Events in a Distributed System" (1978)
**Author**: Leslie Lamport
**Venue**: Communications of the ACM
**Significance**: Most cited paper in distributed systems. Introduced logical clocks and happens-before.

### "The Part-Time Parliament" (1989)
**Author**: Leslie Lamport
**Venue**: ACM TOCS 1998 (written 1989)
**Significance**: Paxos algorithm, presented as fictional Greek parliament.

### "How to Make a Multiprocessor Computer That Correctly Executes Multiprocess Programs" (1979)
**Author**: Leslie Lamport
**Venue**: IEEE TC
**Significance**: Sequential consistency.

## Byzantine Fault Tolerance

### "The Byzantine Generals Problem" (1982)
**Authors**: L. Lamport, R. Shostak, M. Pease
**Venue**: ACM TOPLAS
**Significance**: Classic problem definition and solutions.

## Mutual Exclusion

### "A New Solution of Dijkstra's Concurrent Programming Problem" (1974)
**Author**: Leslie Lamport
**Venue**: CACM
**Significance**: Bakery algorithm.

## TLA+

### "The Temporal Logic of Actions" (1994)
**Author**: Leslie Lamport
**Venue**: ACM TOPLAS
**Significance**: Foundation of TLA+.

### "Specifying Systems" (2002)
**Author**: Leslie Lamport
**Publication**: Addison-Wesley
**Significance**: TLA+ textbook.

## LaTeX Documentation

### "LaTeX: A Document Preparation System" (1986)
**Author**: Leslie Lamport
**Publisher**: Addison-Wesley
**Significance**: Definitive LaTeX reference.
